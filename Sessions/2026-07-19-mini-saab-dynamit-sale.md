# Mini-Run 2026-07-19 - Saab Dynamit Sale

## Summary

After the crew boosted a found **Saab Dynamit 778 TI**, **Curtis** used **Taco** as a vehicle/salvage contact to line up a discreet buyer. Curtis offered Taco a 10 percent cut, pushed for a high price, and settled the sale at **32,000¥**. Taco's cut was **3,200¥**, Finisher's spent SMG ammunition was restocked for **20¥**, and Curtis netted **28,780¥** before voluntarily paying **1,000¥** each to **Mevin**, **Valgaut**, and **Kurgan** as thank-you cuts.

The sale handoff at **612 Wharf Avenue** drew suspicious attention from an Americar that watched the meet and then shadowed Grandpa. Curtis used **Buzz** for surveillance and **The Finisher** for an armed drone response on an exposed freight access road. The Americar was stopped, its occupants were neutralized, and Curtis withdrew in the opposite direction from Taco's compound while Buzz checked for additional tails.

## Major Beats

- Curtis identified the Rigger 3 vehicle as a **Saab Dynamit 778 TI**, a high-end sports car with a listed retail cost of **92,000¥**.
- Taco accepted Curtis's offer of a **10 percent** cut to broker a buyer through his garage/salvage network.
- Curtis prepared the car well enough to support a strong under-table price, but the buyer would not meet Curtis's **35,000¥** ask.
- Taco's buyer agreed to **32,000¥** for the hot vehicle.
- The handoff location was set at a late-night industrial lot around **612 Wharf Avenue**.
- A gray Americar watched the handoff and later followed Grandpa at a distance.
- Curtis used **Buzz** to monitor the tail, then chose an exposed freight access road as an air-attack-friendly location.
- Curtis put Grandpa on autopilot, launched **The Finisher**, and made two SMG attack passes against the Americar.
- The Americar stopped on the freight road; Finisher took no repairable damage, though the return fire forced an ugly correction.
- Curtis drove away from Taco's compound while Buzz checked for other tails.
- Curtis later tried to source **Photovoltaic Chameleon Paint** for Grandpa. Taco could not source a kit immediately, and Curtis's Matrix search failed while creating a small trace/attention risk. The normal next Taco availability check is after **14 days**.

## Mechanical / Economic Results

- Saab sale price: **32,000¥**.
- Taco's 10 percent broker cut: **-3,200¥**.
- Finisher SMG ammunition restock: **-20¥**.
- Curtis's sale net before crew thank-yous: **+28,780¥**.
- Curtis paid **1,000¥** each to **Mevin**, **Valgaut**, and **Kurgan**: **-3,000¥** total.
- Curtis's tracked nuyen after the sale and thank-yous: **25,720¥**.
- The chameleon paint reminder is set for **2026-08-02**, matching the normal **14-day** availability interval.

## Changes to Campaign State

- Curtis's funds increased from the Saab sale and were updated on his page.
- Taco's role as Curtis's vehicle/salvage broker was reinforced.
- The Saab Dynamit 778 TI is no longer in crew possession.
- Grandpa remains unmodified by chameleon paint for now; no kit has been sourced yet.
- A small future attention/trace flag exists from Curtis's failed Matrix search for chameleon paint suppliers.
