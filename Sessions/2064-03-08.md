# Session 2064-03-08

## Summary

Gobang, church-cult, and abandoned-building threads collide as the team plans to trigger a gas evacuation, extract scientists, probe security sites, and uncover corporate / magical weirdness around the job.

## Major Scenes

- Ivan assembles a crew around Mistice and Fong for an extraction involving two scientists held in a trapped / gassed facility.
- Parallel legwork splits between an abandoned building and a suspicious church-cult line.
- The team discovers homeless people used as living vessels / victims in a chrome-and-cult environment.
- The extraction intersects with Gobang, security drones, mercenary posture, and a Wuxing-adjacent delivery chain.

## Clues Gained
