## High-level campaign shape revealed by the archive

The processed archive shows the campaign repeatedly cycling through a few big thematic lanes:

- **identity and imitation** — fake crews, impersonators, manipulated personas, performative celebrities, AI copies
- **corp-tech exploitation** — surveillance implants, analytics harvesting, cloning gear, customer modeling
- **magical contamination** — toxic biomaterial, dragon egg anomalies, insect-spirit processing
- **urban power struggle** — gangs, federal pressure, redevelopment, curfew riots, contested civic space
- **music/media manipulation** — performers, influence engines, Darla/Cindy threads, hype ecosystems
