# Session 2065-03-04

## Summary

The team tears into the Fiona Jones / Bingham 4 mystery, tangles with gang safe houses and Kodachi-linked BTL fallout, and uses [Taco](../NPCs/Taco.md)'s intel network to chase a missing-anchor lead through downtown Nashville.

> QA note: the scanned notes oscillate between near-spellings of Fiona's name; this page standardizes on **Fiona Jones**.

## Major Scenes

- Ivan and Atlas bring the team into the disappearance of Cross News anchor Fiona Jones, who vanished while infiltrating a BTL gang in Bingham 4.
- Legwork with Chinese gang members, safe houses, and rooftop / boarded-building infiltration uncovers Kodachi as a key local power.
- The crew learns Fiona may not be a simple victim; she appears to be operating as part of the gang's ecosystem.
- The pursuit culminates at a station / trailer site where Fiona is grabbed just as a Godzilla-scale creature and a flying mecha monstrosity erupt into the scene.

## Clues Gained

- Cross had already paid off police and was treating Fiona's disappearance as a shadow problem, not a public one.
- Kodachi was the shot-caller in Bingham 4 and had a direct connection to Fiona's infiltration.
- Fiona had done undercover work like this before, which explains some of her apparent gang integration.
- The final confrontation reveals a threat scale far beyond ordinary gang business.

## Decisions Made

- treat the case as an infiltration rescue, not a straight gang hit
- keep captured gang members alive long enough to rat on Kodachi and related locations
- use Taco's feeds, burner phones, and tail work to set the final grab
- snatch Fiona fast once she is exposed in the open

## Changes to Campaign State

- Fiona is pulled back into the team's orbit instead of remaining buried in Bingham 4.
- The campaign's threat scale spikes from local gang violence to kaiju / mecha absurdity in a single beat.
