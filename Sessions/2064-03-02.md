# Session 2064-03-02

## Summary

The team investigates an observatory / initiatory group with heavy magical security, chases Heart-of-Rationality leads, roughs up Arthur, and gathers ritual items to track a deeper occult target.

## Major Scenes

- The team investigates an observatory near Radnor Lake protected by heavy magical security and an initiatory group.
- Research into early shamanic writings and the "Heart of Rationality" points toward a ritual network and a hidden authorial identity.
- Arthur is followed, ambushed, and stripped of ritual leverage while the crew seizes items tied to the working.
- Security, ritual remains, and Ming Ma-linked protection around the site suggest the operation is bigger than a single mage cell.

## Clues Gained

- The observatory group is using sympathetic links and coordinated chanting for serious ritual work.
- The "Heart of Rationality" appears to be a pen name rather than a straightforward text attribution.
- Early shamanic writings may amplify magical power in ways worth killing over.
- The site is guarded by both mundane weapons and ritual infrastructure.
