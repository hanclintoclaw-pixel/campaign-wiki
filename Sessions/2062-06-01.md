# Session 2062-06-01

## Summary

Ivan and Atlas pull the team into a Renraku Arcology-era mess centered on a young G.O.D.-linked target, floor-by-floor arcology intel, Deus-worshipping survivors, invasive cyberware, and evidence of AI-tainted escapees.

## Major Scenes

- Ivan and Atlas bring the team a Renraku Arcology-linked investigation with G.O.D. involvement and a suspicious target on floor 83.
- The crew scouts the building's vertical layout, splits responsibilities, and enters a hostile cyber / cult environment.
- Inside, they find lifeless people in beds, spider drones, and a pseudo-religious Deus-worshipping system presence.
- The run turns into a desperate extraction of bodies, cyberware evidence, data, and survivors while the system closes in.
- The team escapes with evidence that Arcology artifacts and AI-tainted code may already be leaking into the outside world.

## Clues Gained

- A high-value target may be tied directly to the Renraku Arcology shutdown and a 16-minute surveillance lapse.
- Floor 83 and upper research / residence levels hide more than simple corporate secrecy.
- The site includes active Delta-grade cyberware capability and evidence of medical / body experimentation.
- Escaped Arcology actors may be carrying cyberware, AI code, and other dangerous remnants into the wider world.
- There may be a control-code / resurrection angle tied to hostile AI continuity.

## Decisions Made

- accept the G.O.D.-linked recon / extraction job
- split the team across scouting, hardware, and physical-response roles
- exfiltrate data and material evidence rather than leaving the mystery contained on-site
- destroy or sever active links when escape becomes the priority

## Changes to Campaign State

- The campaign gains a direct Renraku Arcology / Deus contamination thread.
- The team acquires evidence that AI-tainted fallout is already outside the Arcology.
- G.O.D. and Arcology survivors become much more relevant to future runs.
