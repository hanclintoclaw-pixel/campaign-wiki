# Session 2065-04-08

## Summary

A casino / penthouse infiltration spirals from Domino Boxcar's setup through Kilamanjaro's access sell, astral trouble around Withered Richie, a Mob Mood riot, and a hard-drive-and-body cleanup on the 30th floor.

## Major Scenes

- The crew attends orientation for the casino job and receives a small box from Domino Boxcar.
- Kilamanjaro and under-the-table access deals get the team moving toward the upper floors and penthouse-security layer.
- [Glenn](../NPCs/Glenn.md) tracks an astral trail to the CEO, a monstrous / withered Richie figure, and gets carved up in the process.
- A riot, concierge-station wreckage, and 30th-floor cleanup lead to the discovery of a dying Howard Hughes type kept alive in a bed.
- Domino begs for his life, Kilamanjaro shoots him, and the team wipes cameras, stashes the body, and leaves with cash, chips, and hard drives.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- Domino Boxcar
- Kilamanjaro
- [Glenn](../NPCs/Glenn.md)
- Withered Richie [name uncertain]
- unnamed penthouse invalid / Howard Hughes type figure

## Clues Gained

- The casino's upper levels hide both occult hostility and a grotesque executive secret.
- Kilamanjaro is willing to sell access and then solve problems with cold execution.
- Hard drives and security recordings are valuable enough to justify a full cleanup instead of a simple smash-and-grab.

## Decisions Made

- buy the penthouse key instead of trying to force the 29th-floor barrier blind
- keep pushing upward after Glenn is wounded
- wipe security footage and hide the body trail before extraction
- take the money, drugs, chips, and data rather than leaving anything recoverable behind

## Changes to Campaign State

- The casino arc pays off in both nuyen and sensitive data.
- Domino Boxcar is dead, and Kilamanjaro proves himself ruthlessly decisive.
- The team removes a major chunk of visible evidence from the site.
