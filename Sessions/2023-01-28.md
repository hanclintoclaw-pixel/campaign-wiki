# Session 2023-01-28

## Summary

The crew took a grim retrieval job involving missing employee **Genevieve Watson** and uncovered evidence that she had been secretly fitted with surveillance cyberware for covert recording and data extraction.

## Major Scenes

- Floyd Otis offered payment for Genevieve Watson's return, with disturbingly specific interest in recovering her cyberware intact.
- The team determined the job was really about recovering hardware and recorded data, not helping Genevieve.
- The crew visited Genevieve's apartment and learned hostile parties had already searched the room for data-enabled equipment.
- They established contact with coworker **Candace Wrapper**.
- The surveillance implants were understood to involve hidden recording hardware and a trigger/upload mechanism through a specific datajack connection.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- Genevieve Watson
- Floyd Otis
- Dr. Waters
- Candace Wrapper

## Clues Gained

- Genevieve had likely been implanted without informed consent
- her eyes and ears were rigged for covert recording
- hostile parties were searching for the extracted data trail
- Floyd Otis was concealing medical details about the implants

## Decisions Made

- investigate the data trail and medical connection instead of treating the job as a simple bounty retrieval

## Changes to Campaign State

- the crew became entangled in a corporate/medical surveillance exploitation plot
