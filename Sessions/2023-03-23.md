# Session 2023-03-23

## Summary

The crew was hired to protect a peace meeting between two rival gun-running gangs: the Interstate Connection and the Ironblood Orphans.

## Major Scenes

- Crew learned the structure and stakes of the proposed gang peace meeting.
- Mevin traced interest in the meeting to the Federal Matrix Investigators in Atlanta.
- The team met with March Hare of the Interstate Connection to negotiate security expectations.
- The team met with Lacey Rational of the Ironblood Orphans and reviewed her proposed territorial/business settlement.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- March Hare
- Lacey Rational
- Herrick / Harac

## Clues Gained

- Federal Matrix Investigators were unexpectedly interested in local gun-running dynamics.
- Neither gang appeared to be openly flying the Humanis flag.
- Lacey Rational appeared to be hiding some outside-threat information.

## Decisions Made

- reduce the number of bodyguards near the actual leaders
- structure the meeting around layered physical security and anti-third-party interference
- communicate expectations to both gangs rather than treating either side as automatically trustworthy

## Changes to Campaign State

- Herrick/Harac's value as a contact node was reinforced.
- Federal interest in local gang dynamics became a live concern.

## Open Threads

- why the Federal Matrix Investigators were watching this
- whether the gangs would actually make peace
- what outside party might intervene
