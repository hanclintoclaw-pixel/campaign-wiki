# Session 2022-08-11

## Summary

The crew investigated leaks from the Gladwell Institute rehab facility and narrowed suspicion onto security chief **Eugene Booker**, while uncovering hints that he might be frightened, manipulated, or set up rather than acting alone.

## Major Scenes

- The team was hired to find and stop the source leaking embarrassing celebrity rehab stories.
- Suspicion centered on **Dr. Martin Waters**, **Sally Eaton**, and eventually **Eugene Booker**.
- The crew tailed Booker, probed his security profile, and began mapping his apartment with a spider drone.
- Mevin investigated Booker's old matrix-gang history and found surprisingly little public trace.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- Dr. Charles Weaver
- Dr. Martin Waters
- Sally Eaton
- Eugene Booker

## Clues Gained

- the leaks targeted prominent metahuman-rights figures
- Booker looked nervous and hunted rather than comfortably guilty
- his sealed/obscure gang past suggested missing context or active cleanup

## Decisions Made

- investigate whether Booker was being used or framed rather than just burned as the obvious culprit

## Changes to Campaign State

- the Gladwell Institute leak case shifted from a simple internal security matter toward a more layered conspiracy

## Open Threads

- whether Booker was responsible, coerced, or being prepared as the fall guy
- who else at Gladwell benefited from the leaks
- what role, if any, magic-based detox or awakened services played in the case
