# Session 2064-04-14

## Summary

Johnny the PI, Soldador politics, and a convoy / upper-floor mystery push [Mucky](../NPCs/Mucky.md) and Mike toward a horrifying chempera-style creature and a deeply wrong hidden layer inside the target building.

## Major Scenes

- The crew digs deeper into the Soldador / Kluge problem and meets with local power brokers about a paid nuisance operation that has to stop.
- John Cantoo / "The Amazing Johnny" enters the picture as a mystic / investigator-style contact.
- Surveillance around the motel and upper floors reveals signs of something monstrous, blocked senses, and a horrific non-animal chem-creature.
- Mike and Mucky probe the site further while wrestling with magical feedback, distorted perception, and BTL-adjacent weirdness.

- The site appears to blend gang business, occult contamination, and sensory interference.
