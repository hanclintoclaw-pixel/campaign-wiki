# Session 2065-09-01

## Summary

A maintenance-worker infiltration into an old-airport / animal-facility job turns into a grab for a dangerous captive creature, with drone support, anti-magic complications, tranquilizers, and a fast van extraction.

## Major Scenes

- The team infiltrates a facility under maintenance-worker cover with instructions to move the live prize to an old airport.
- The site presents as a mix of zoo / wildlife / exotic-animal infrastructure with badge access and internal alarms.
- [Glenn](../NPCs/Glenn.md) works the upper floors, maintenance access, and spider-drone angles while anti-magic plant material and tranquilizers come into play.
- The team breaches containment, sedates the target, and hauls it to the van despite security response.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- [Glenn](../NPCs/Glenn.md)
- [Kurgan](../PCs/Kurgan.md)
- Toro / Toro buzzes building [name uncertain]

## Clues Gained

- The target is important enough to require covert animal-handling logistics rather than a standard smash-and-grab.
- Anti-magic plant material is being used or stored alongside the target.
- Maintenance access is a far cleaner path into the site than public-facing entrances.

## Decisions Made

- use maintenance-worker cover for initial penetration
- rely on tranquilizers and drone scouting instead of brute force at the start
- extract the live target to the van as soon as the breach window opens

## Changes to Campaign State

- The crew successfully adds another strange live-capture / live-transport operation to its history.
- The campaign's blend of magic suppression, exotic fauna, and covert extraction deepens again.
