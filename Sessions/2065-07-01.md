# Session 2065-07-01

## Summary

The team hits an urban-blight salvage and retrieval war, discovers Ares-grade weapons and gear caches, and survives a tactical strike-up with drones and organized shooters around the Milton East target zone.

## Major Scenes

- A Mr. Johnson hires the team to break a gang takeover in Milton East / an urban-blight zone organized around multiple central entrances.
- Salvageable convoy gear, new chrome, and unreleased weapons make the site more attractive and more dangerous.
- [Taco](../NPCs/Taco.md) identifies real small-unit tactics under the gang's posture, confirming the takeover is more disciplined than it first appears.
- The assault ends with Ares retrieval threats, brutal overwatch kills, fleeing defenders, and a major recovered weapons cache.

## Clues Gained

- The gang is sitting on Ares-linked high-value weaponry, not just street guns.
- Former military / dishonorably discharged personnel may be part of the leadership layer.
- The cache includes advanced assault rifles, set guns, handguns, cash, and an autodoc.

## Decisions Made

- hit the takeover hard rather than negotiate around it
- secure the supply / storage apartment as the key node of the operation
- keep pressure on overwatch and street guards until the rest scatter

## Changes to Campaign State

- The team strips a major weapons node out of Milton East.
- Ares high-value-weapon attention becomes a live factor instead of background flavor.
