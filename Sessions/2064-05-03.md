# Session 2064-05-03

## Summary

Hired to keep riggers alive on a magnetically scrambled mutant island, the team navigates sentinels, infected / druid logic, bizarre X-Men-flavored threats, and escalating combat around the riggers' survival.

## Major Scenes

- The crew is hired to keep riggers alive on an island cut off by magnetic interference, blocked communications, and impossible satellite coverage.
- Sentinel craft, mutant-making phenomena, and black-castle / other-dimension weirdness turn the mission into a nightmare survival op.
- [Mucky](../NPCs/Mucky.md) leads some of the most aggressive field action, including a juggernaut kill and repeated pushes through impossible terrain.
- The run widens into ghosts, awakened plants, Heavenly Host questions, Xing security handoffs, and citywide magical pressure.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- [Mucky](../NPCs/Mucky.md)
- [Professor X](../NPCs/Professor-X.md)
- Xing head coach / security contacts
- Heavenly Host figures including The White Blur and Torgo [as reported targets]

## Clues Gained

- The island's interference is strong enough to defeat normal comms and orbital observation.
- Mutant / infection phenomena on-site are tied to deeper magical contamination, not just weird biology.
- Sentinel movement patterns, black-castle imagery, and dimensional ward openings point to a layered threat environment.
- Heavenly Host, awakened plants, and matrix-cutoff contingencies suggest a much bigger conspiracy beyond the island job.

## Decisions Made

- commit to keeping the riggers alive instead of abandoning the island objective
- use sentinel timing, patrol routes, and physical aggression to force progress through the site
- keep pressing investigation even after the job mutates into a broader magical / mutant crisis

## Changes to Campaign State

- The campaign's mutant / X-Men-flavored arc becomes one of the strangest and most expansive threat webs on the board.
- Xing security and Heavenly Host actors now sit much closer to the action.
- The team comes away with both money and a pile of new unresolved supernatural intelligence.

## Open Threads

- why awakened plants and matrix-cutoff planning sit inside the same ecosystem
- whether the black-castle / dimensional imagery was symbolic, real, or both
- what Xing security is really moving in those containers
