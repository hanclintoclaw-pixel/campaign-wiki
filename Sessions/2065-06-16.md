# Session 2065-06-16

## Summary

A Wilson Phillips producer extraction / robbery run mixes penthouse access, forged badges, [Taco](../NPCs/Taco.md)-truck surveillance, music-industry side players, and a follow-through tail on security details and unmarked storefronts.

## Major Scenes

- A restaurant meet and cone-of-silence briefing set up a job targeting music producer Wilson Phillips.
- The crew builds access through badges, workstation tricks, social engineering, and surveillance around Phillips's penthouse and security detail.
- [Glenn](../NPCs/Glenn.md), Mevin, [Kurgan](../PCs/Kurgan.md), and Taco chase a moving target through storefronts, a taco operation, and city-side tails.
- The run evolves toward an airport / transit interception plan with a Faraday-bag-style extraction concept.

## Clues Gained

- Wilson Phillips is surrounded by a real six-person security package including at least one mage.
- He appears connected to sub-BTL or adjacent entertainment-market exploitation.
- His routes and contacts run through low-profile storefronts and disguised business fronts rather than obvious celebrity venues.
- The airport / smuggler-side angle may be the cleanest interception point.

## Decisions Made

- attack the problem through access, surveillance, and tail work instead of a frontal penthouse raid
- steal badges and admin access to widen the team's options
- prepare to bag Phillips electrically / electronically for transport once isolated

## Changes to Campaign State

- Wilson Phillips becomes a concrete live target instead of a vague industry rumor.
- The team tightens its entertainment-industry extraction toolkit yet again.
