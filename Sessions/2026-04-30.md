# Session 2026-04-30

## Summary

The crew followed up on their capture of Jet Set Morgan. The street hustler was revealed to be a gig-economy stooge working for the same employer who had previously tasked the Street Promoter/Gargoyle with recording and distributing footage of Darla Ledoux. Analysis of Morgan's van revealed a second satellite receiver, while examination of his cyberterminal showed it had been root-kitted in a way that let this employer exert deep control over Morgan's life.

Pursuing that lead, the crew tracked down another member of the same meat-drone network and gathered information suggesting that Cindy Lou may be the copy rather than the newly encountered SA-Knowbot. During that follow-up, the team received counterfeit Augmented Beef and Bacon Social Club vending snacks. Valgaut's Magical Sense indicated the food was adulterated with unknown magical creature matter, and Kurgan found the name "Mother Yahweh" written on one of the boxes.

## Major Scenes

- The crew interrogated Jet Set Morgan after his capture.
- Curtis examined the satellite hardware while Mevin worked the software angle.
- Mevin decked into the relay satellite and planted a tracker to activate the next time the mysterious employer reaches out.
- The crew met another delivery-network participant and obtained counterfeit snack products tied to the Darla Ledoux promotional push.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- Jet Set Morgan
- Unnamed gig-economy delivery man
- Mother Yahweh (name discovered on packaging; identity still unresolved)

## Clues Gained

- The rootkit on Jet Set Morgan's cyberterminal has architecture similar to Cindy Lou Jenkins's data structures, but appears more primitive.
- For roughly a month, the satellite-linked employer has been directing people to promote Darla Ledoux, including recording concerts, stealing music, and manipulating replacement/distribution activity.
- Some Meatlets, Bebop Colas, and other snacks tied to the upcoming Darla Ledoux ad blitz are adulterated with unknown magical ingredients.
- The name "Mother Yahweh" is connected in some way to the counterfeit snack pipeline.
- Evidence now points toward a possible rogue SA-Knowbot being involved in the album-theft operation.

## Decisions Made

- Release Jet Set Morgan and convert him from a disposable target into a Level 1 contact for Kurgan.
- Maintain both satellite dishes for interception and keep the comm-sat tracing utility in place to identify the mysterious employer when they reconnect.
- Continue treating the adulterated snack angle as a live threat tied to the broader Darla operation.

## Changes to Campaign State

- Jet Set Morgan was released and is now a potential Level 1 contact for Kurgan.
- The crew is now confident that a rogue or rival SA-Knowbot may be behind the Darla Ledoux album theft, though motive remains unclear.
- The Darla promotional campaign is now linked not only to influence operations and theft, but also to tainted consumables with magical contamination.
- "Mother Yahweh" entered the campaign as a named but unresolved lead.
