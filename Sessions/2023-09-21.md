# Session 2023-09-21

## Summary

After the retreat of the federal airship, the crew helped defend Dr. Gallstone's veterinary practice during a curfew riot inside an arcoblock and managed crowd control through intimidation, improvisation, and spectacle.

## Major Scenes

- The group responded to Dr. Gallstone's request for help protecting his practice.
- Glenn improvised security from Taco's gear while Mevin manipulated the local building network.
- The crew established control over the 17th floor crowd through intimidation, fake hospitality, dancing, and magical/social crowd management.
- The team then moved up to the 18th floor and found a matrix-heavy party floor with serious damage and a separate local network setup.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- Dr. Gallstone
- Bruce Gallstone
- Hidden Enforcers contact (unnamed)

## Clues Gained

- the federal airship's retreat had left behind a scavenging wave and unstable civic mood
- the mayor's curfew policy was shaping local unrest
- the 18th floor housed an unexpectedly sophisticated matrix environment with deep-dive beds and satellite decking chairs

## Decisions Made

- defend the veterinary office as a favor to Dr. Gallstone
- use social control and spectacle rather than immediate lethal force on the crowd
- extend the effort from the 17th floor to the more chaotic 18th floor

## Changes to Campaign State

- Dr. Gallstone became a more strongly established favor/contact node
- post-federal-exit civic instability became a major lived campaign condition

## Open Threads

- what exactly the 18th-floor matrix bar was for
- how long the curfew unrest would continue
- how the city would stabilize after the federal retreat
