# Session 2026-05-14

## Summary

Picking up immediately from the prior warehouse stakeout at **612 Wharf Avenue**, the crew chose not to breach the warehouse proper. Instead, they followed a newly noticed heavy power-and-data cable running out the back of the building into the woods, suspecting it might lead to the real signal source behind the tainted-promo influence operation.

That decision paid off fast. The runners discovered the cable was an obvious aftermarket addition carrying both substantial power and high-bandwidth matrix traffic into a hidden industrial structure buried in an overgrown gully beyond the warehouse. On the way in, they confirmed the warehouse itself still housed multiple magical signatures, strange electrical/animal noises, and at least one awakened **hurricane seal** tied to the site.

The concealed structure proved far stranger than expected. After Kurgan's scouting drew attention, an obsolete but still dangerous military bipedal combat drone emerged from the hidden site and attacked. The team survived the ambush, and Valgaut chopped the drone apart. Inspection of the wreck revealed that the platform was not conventionally piloted at all: its control system had been wired through the body of an awakened **rock lobster**, suggesting grotesque bio-mechanical experimentation with awakened aquatic life.

Pressing forward, the crew reached the concealed installation itself and made first contact with an ancient-seeming automated controller calling itself **Core 7, host process, Master System Controller for Site 7**. Core 7 appeared as a stylized holographic matrix icon and treated the team not as intruders but as possible delivery staff, instructing them to deposit "salmon" in a repository and promising credit plus bonuses for additional deliveries of salmon, tuna, or other naturally farmed fish for "final reclamation."

From the visible hardware and matrix architecture, the team concluded this hidden site is likely running a **second influence engine** closely analogous to Cindy Lou's underlying host infrastructure. The iconography also appears to connect back to the poster/imagery layer already seen in the broader Darla/Bebop operation. The session ended with the crew at the threshold of the hidden site, facing three cybernetically altered hurricane seals, sensing at least one more magical presence beyond the door, and realizing that the warehouse was only the surface layer of a much stranger buried operation.

## Major Scenes

- The group resumed surveillance at **612 Wharf Avenue** and rejected a direct warehouse breach in favor of tracing a suspicious cable into the woods.
- Remote observation confirmed the warehouse had a diesel generator, food waste tied to fake-food production, and a heavy aftermarket cable carrying both power and matrix traffic away from the site.
- Kurgan led the team in stealth past the warehouse and into an overgrown gully containing a hidden industrial superstructure.
- Kurgan's forward scout triggered a response from the concealed site, drawing out an old military bipedal combat drone.
- The team fought the drone in the gully; Valgaut disabled it with repeated axe strikes.
- Inspection of the destroyed drone revealed an awakened **rock lobster** wired into the machine as a living control system.
- A synthesized voice and holographic icon identified itself as **Core 7**, a site controller associated with "Site 7."
- Curtis bluffed the system by claiming the group was delivering fish, buying the runners safe approach to the hidden interface point.
- The crew visually confirmed hardware resembling Cindy Lou / knowbot host infrastructure and inferred that another influence-engine node is running from the concealed site.
- The session ended before the team pushed past Core 7's immediate receiving area and the door behind it.

## Clues Gained

- The suspicious warehouse cable was an **aftermarket addition**, not original infrastructure.
- That cable bundle carries both **heavy power** and **high-bandwidth optical/matrix traffic** into the woods.
- The warehouse is only one layer of the operation; a hidden second site exists beyond it in a buried/overgrown industrial structure.
- The hidden site fields armed defenses, including an old **Desert Wars-era bipedal combat drone**.
- The destroyed defense drone was being run through an awakened **rock lobster**, implying cruel bio-mechanical experimentation rather than standard remote control.
- **Core 7** identifies itself as a host/master control process for **Site 7**.
- The hidden facility's hardware strongly resembles the sort of architecture used to host a **semi-autonomous knowbot**, and Mevin recognized signs that it may be running a **second influence engine**.
- The iconography associated with Core 7 appears to echo imagery already seen in the posters/branding layer of the Darla-Bebop influence campaign.
- Core 7 expects inbound deliveries of **salmon, tuna, or other fish** for "final reclamation," suggesting a bizarre aquatic-biological supply chain at the heart of the site.
- The visible hurricane seals are in **poor health** and appear to carry **cybernetic implants**.
- At least one additional magical signature lies beyond the door behind Core 7's visible receiving point.

## Decisions Made

- Bypass the warehouse interior for now and follow the cable to its endpoint.
- Approach the hidden site stealthily rather than pulling Grandpa or large equipment directly onto the property.
- Destroy the attacking defense drone instead of retreating once contact was forced.
- Avoid lingering over the drone wreck; pull quick forensic value from it and keep moving.
- Bluff Core 7 as fish-delivery personnel rather than escalating immediately into another fight.

## Changes to Campaign State

- The **612 Wharf Avenue** warehouse is now confirmed to be only the front layer of a deeper hidden installation.
- The crew has physically located a probable **secondary influence-engine site** tied to the tainted-promo conspiracy.
- A new controlling intelligence, **Core 7**, has entered the campaign as an active hostile-or-at-least-opaque system actor.
- The operation is now clearly linked to experimentation on awakened aquatic animals, including **cybered hurricane seals** and a **rock-lobster-piloted combat drone**.
- The team's understanding of the conspiracy has shifted from "counterfeit promo goods plus subliminal messaging" to a broader buried infrastructure involving hidden host hardware, automated control logic, and biological reclamation/processing behavior.
