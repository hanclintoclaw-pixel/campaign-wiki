# Session 2065-05-01

## Summary

The crew collides with zombie-making tech, songwriter theft, chop-shop / roller-rink violence, gang-side politics, and Devil Dogs pressure while trying to contain a bizarre escalating machine-driven mess.

## Major Scenes

- The crew tangles with a songwriting / software machine that seems to create from the dead while zombifying them in the process.
- Merle, [Taco](../NPCs/Taco.md), [Herrick](../NPCs/Herrick.md), [Mucky](../NPCs/Mucky.md), [Kurgan](../PCs/Kurgan.md), and Malaise all get pulled into overlapping tech, chop-shop, and roller-crew fallout.
- Aaron Brand and Heaven's Myst / Mist become central to the occult-tech side of the mess.
- The session culminates in portal fire, a goat-man confrontation, and Aaron Brand's death as books and software secrets turn to dust.

## Clues Gained

- The machine's creative output is tied to death and undeath rather than simple piracy.
- Gary Lee's family / father material points toward binding, inheritance, or constructed legacy.
- Heaven's Myst / Mist appears to connect the tech problem to a more explicitly magical one.
- Aaron Brand is not just a bystander; he is deep in the core secret web.

## Decisions Made

- destroy or interrupt the zombie-producing machine rather than exploit it
- chase the tech / chop-shop side of the operation hard enough to flush out Aaron Brand
- follow the portal / occult escalation instead of cutting losses early

## Changes to Campaign State

- Aaron Brand is dead.
- Key books / software artifacts are destroyed or rendered inaccessible.
- The team leaves with a strange key and a lingering obligation tied to feeding something every day.
