# Session 2062-02-08

## Summary

Concert-season chaos escalates as the team clashes with drones, Roach, T-Dog, Luther, and Hank IT threads, juggles Vandy / hermetic-book leads, and tries to keep control while the festival operation spirals toward violence.

## Major Scenes

- With the concert approaching, the team races to stop a multi-site ork / ghoul / Roach-linked attack plan.
- A van ambush and industrial firefight erupts around drones, heavy vehicles, and a rescue / escape scramble.
- Hank IT remains central as the crew interrogates captives, tracks Roach, and tries to hold the situation together.
- Luther, T-Dog, Hidden Enforcers, and Aries Stadium all converge into the same escalating threat picture.
- The concert climax ends in a stadium push, arrests, recovered orichalcum, and a final warning broadcast.

## Clues Gained

- The concert unrest is meant to draw police off while other sites are hit.
- Roach appears to be controlling or influencing multiple actors in the operation.
- A hermetic thesis / book and ritual framework may connect to the broader magic threat.
- Ghouls are queuing around the stadium and the conspiracy is tied to the concert schedule itself.
- The team ultimately recovers a large orichalcum haul and hears Hank IT say the message is in his recordings.

## Decisions Made

- lean into aggressive field response instead of waiting out the concert plot
- interrogate prisoners and keep chasing Roach's network
- involve outside magical help where possible, including anti-Roach / binding angles
- commit to a direct stadium intervention once the threat picture sharpens

## Changes to Campaign State

- T-Dog and Sweetums are arrested with the remaining orichalcum actors.
- The team walks away with roughly 25 kg of orichalcum.
- Merle signals full payout and offers additional upside tied to Chrome Rose.
