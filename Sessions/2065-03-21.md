# Session 2065-03-21

## Summary

A reopened [Floyd Otis](../NPCs/Floyd-Otis.md) / souvenir-store security job turns into protest management, hidden backers, and odd reveal beats as the crew uncovers off-book financing, suspicious cargo, and a very strange gorilla-balloon payoff.

## Major Scenes

- A Johnson job brokered through Ivan pulls the crew into security work around the reopening of the Otis souvenir store.
- Protesters, armored overwatch, and an RV-linked movement pattern suggest the storefront is cover for something more important.
- Interrogation and legwork expose paid protesters, Bainbridge, and a ZRT-backed financial / logistical angle tied to Floyd Otis's family.
- The notes then pivot into planning for a separate casino / penthouse job, showing the crew stacking operations back to back.

## Clues Gained

- The protest movement around Otis is at least partly manufactured and financed.
- A heavy object / container was delivered off the books by crane.
- ZRT appears to be bankrolling Floyd Otis's nephew and exerting influence behind the scenes.
- The store security problem may hide a magical / spirit angle as well as a mundane political one.

## Decisions Made

- accept the security contract despite its messy public-facing optics
- interrogate paid protesters rather than treat the crowd as a single bloc
- open and inspect the suspicious delivered object once the team has a chance
- begin parallel prep for the follow-on casino / penthouse operation

## Changes to Campaign State

- Floyd Otis's orbit becomes more politically and corporately entangled.
- The crew shifts into a rhythm of layered contracts, with one security problem already bleeding into the next heist-style dossier.

## Open Threads

- why the crane-delivered object mattered so much
- how the casino job will intersect with the same power web, if at all
