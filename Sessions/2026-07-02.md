# Session 2026-07-02

## Summary

The crew concluded the two-session **Griswell Data Services / Alvin Flang** surveillance run. After preserving the tail from **Music Circle**, they traced the mystery watcher to **Palermo**, a paydata broker tied to the **Ruby Falls data haven** and known around Nashville for corporate information work. The follow carried the team from surface-level corporate surveillance into an underground squatter market below the city, where Palermo maintained a protected hideout with hidden utility and Matrix connections. The crew confronted him professionally rather than burning the site down, secured a burner identity and enough proof to satisfy the contract, and turned the job in cleanly: **60,000¥ total, 15,000¥ each, and 6 karma each**.

## Major Scenes

- The session opened by re-establishing the end of the prior run: the crew had followed the polished watcher from **Music Circle** toward a waiting self-driving classic car.
- **Mevin** began a long Matrix search for other registrations and traces sharing the same thin corporate / data-broker fingerprint.
- The search connected the watcher to **Palermo**, a known broker of paydata and corporate information operating through the **Ruby Falls data haven** in Chattanooga.
- **Kurgan** recognized Palermo from older **Humanis**-adjacent business: higher-ups had once used him as a source or intermediary for information.
- The team waited through the next Tuesday/Thursday lunch pattern and watched **Alvin Flang** and **Diego** leave **Griswell Data Services** like clockwork.
- At the lunch meet, the crew saw Alvin pass a chip or similar physical data token through the watcher/drone exchange pattern.
- The crew shifted from Alvin to Palermo, following the watcher into a grimier underground tunnel system and squatter market.
- **Kurgan**, **Valgaut**, and **Mevin** adopted disguises and entered on foot while keeping **Grandpa** nearby as the support vehicle.
- **Valgaut** detected multiple awakened presences ahead; the team identified several **devil rats** living unusually close to the tunnel population.
- Local rat wranglers were apparently being paid to keep the devil rats around as living guard animals that discouraged outsiders from approaching Palermo's area.
- **Kurgan** spotted disguised power and Matrix utility connections feeding the central structure, confirming the hideout was not just a squatter shack.
- The crew chose a professional pressure approach: Mevin talked while Kurgan and Valgaut made the threat obvious.
- Palermo admitted that he finds weak links like Alvin for a living. He framed the Griswell operation as opportunistic exploitation, not a personal vendetta against Griswell.
- Palermo surrendered a burner identity useful for proving the downstream buyer, understood Griswell/Alvin were now off-limits, and likely abandoned the hideout after the crew left.
- The crew turned in the contract successfully and received **60,000¥ total**, split **15,000¥ each**, plus **6 karma each**.

## Discoveries

- The mystery watcher was tied to **Palermo**, a data broker rather than a conventional corporate field agent.
- Palermo has a presence on the **Ruby Falls data haven** and listings for corporate information in and around the Nashville market.
- **Alvin Flang** appears to have been a weak link Palermo exploited, rather than the mastermind of the leak.
- The buyer side of the job used a blend of physical handoff, drone-assisted surveillance, thin corporate registration, and hidden Matrix infrastructure.
- A squatter market below the city includes a Palermo-linked protected structure with hidden utility and Matrix lines.
- **Devil rats** are present in that underground space and are being tolerated or managed as guard animals.

## Consequences

- The **Griswell Data Services** job is concluded successfully.
- The crew now has Palermo's burner identity as the proof-chain needed to satisfy the client.
- Palermo can potentially be cultivated later as a paydata broker contact, though he is untrustworthy and likely to burn compromised locations immediately.
- **Alvin Flang** remains a weak-link security problem, but he is no longer the active endpoint of the crew's job.
- The underground market and devil-rat guard setup are now established parts of the Nashville shadow landscape.
- **Curtis** was not active at table for the conclusion, but the final payout and karma were awarded to include him.
