# Session 2023-11-09

## Summary

After finishing a creativity-theft scam run, the crew took a job to shut down the Pie-town Happening, a magically-charged street fair protected by the Uruk Hai and linked to skooma distribution.

## Major Scenes

- Crew finished up the previous run involving "creativity vampires" draining creative essence.
- A new job offered 40k¥ to shut down the Pie-town Happening and another 20k¥ to identify the skooma supplier.
- The team entered the event, scoped its physical security, and identified a red van as the likely security hub.
- Mevin identified suspicious underground access routes and considered exploring them with a drone.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- Uruk Hai go-gang
- unnamed employer / living avatar of the contracting party

## Clues Gained

- the Pie-town Happening generated a positive magical vibe rather than a hostile background count
- the Uruk Hai were unusually close to the city center
- skooma supply and event protection were linked
- hidden tunnels under the block did not match official records

## Decisions Made

- infiltrate the Happening as paying entrants
- trace the physical security network and hidden infrastructure

## Changes to Campaign State

- the crew moved into a new arc involving magical cultural spaces, gang protection, and drug-distribution infrastructure

## Open Threads

- who was funding the event
- where the skooma was actually coming from
- what the underground tunnels were for
