# Session 2061-12-17

## Summary

The team juggles ghoul-book prophecy fallout, Drexler-compound business, Atlas side favors, and the setup for an Agritec data-drive extraction while a string of bizarre body-horror consequences unfolds around their targets.

- The team works through action / extraction logistics while SURGE and Halley's Comet dominate the background.
- A ghoul-book prophecy is deciphered, analyzed, and reburied on Drexler's old compound.
- Atlas asks the crew to ruin Walter Hopkins, leading to a grotesque supernatural payback operation.
- The group pivots into planning an Agritec data-drive extraction under business-investor cover identities.

## Clues Gained

- The ghoul book describes prophecy, sacrifice, and binding a soul into the material world.
- Rat Shaman analysis flags the book as bad-energy, low-arcane-focus material.
- Walter Hopkins is vulnerable to pressure through his property / inspector scam.
- Agritec's facility sits between Nashville and Knoxville and may hide more than a simple corporate document run.
- Missing-person and special-project indicators point to deeper danger inside Agritec.

## Decisions Made

- accept Atlas's favor-job against Walter Hopkins
