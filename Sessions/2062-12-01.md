# Session 2062-12-01

## Summary

The team tackles an Ork Championship Wrestling fix built around New Jack, a crooked promoter / manager circle, spirit-assisted cheating, and a plan to control the title belt through fake-official chaos.

## Major Scenes

- The team plans an Ork Championship Wrestling infiltration centered on the heel champ, New Jack.
- The job revolves around public humiliation / replacement dynamics, title-belt control, and ringside manipulation.
- The notes sketch a fake-referee angle and a battle-royale setup as the preferred path into the promotion.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- New Jack
- Killer Kawalski
- Verge Gagnie
- Howard Finkle
- Shocklester
- Yeti

## Clues Gained

- New Jack is a brutal, heavily modified heel champion with a cultivated reputation for maiming opponents.
- Killer Kawalski, his manager, is associated with magical fire-spirit support.
- Food / drink tampering and referee manipulation are both viable ways to swing the match environment.
- The promotion itself appears corruptible enough to support a staged infiltration.

## Decisions Made

- infiltrate the wrestling promotion rather than confront it head-on
- install or control a fake referee as part of the plan
- use the battle royale / title-match structure to isolate the real target

## Changes to Campaign State

- OCW wrestling becomes an active campaign theater instead of background color.
- The team adds another social-performance infiltration style to its playbook.

## Open Threads

- who exactly commissioned the New Jack operation
- whether the belt, the champ, or the promotion itself is the real target
- how much magical interference Killer Kawalski can actually bring to ringside
