# Session 2062-02-02

## Summary

Cambien, Hank IT, Sarit, and festival-security threads collide as Efficient Solutions tries to locate, evaluate, contain, and either deliver or destroy a dangerous target while Riverfront-Bar politics and handler intel pile up.

## Major Scenes

- Riverfront Bar maneuvering and Cambien's social plays push the Hank IT operation into motion.
- The team positions Franco on security, digs into Hank's handler chain, and executes the grab.
- Hank is captured, hidden, and medically stabilized while the crew studies what is wrong with him.
- Hidden Enforcers, T-Dog, Roach, and stadium-side conspiracy threads begin converging.
- Aries Stadium is bugged and investigated as a likely centerpiece of a much larger plan.

## Clues Gained

- Hank appears tainted / altered and may have been bitten or transformed by an outside force.
- Hidden Enforcers are planning coordinated hits tied to veteran orks, substituted children, and a concert night.
- Aries inventory includes high-end cyberware, trace orichalcum, BTL/drug stock, and electronics.
- The ultimate target may involve orichalcum and a "spirit bomb" under or near the stadium.
- A statue / structure under the stadium may be intended to rise during the event.

## Decisions Made

- seize Hank and keep him under watch instead of leaving him in enemy hands
- keep pursuing T-Dog and Hidden Enforcers as linked threats
- bug and surveil Aries Stadium rather than treating it as background scenery
- work the problem as a multi-night conspiracy rather than a single grab-and-go

## Changes to Campaign State

- Hank is now an active captive / witness instead of just a rumor.
- The festival / concert line escalates into a city-scale threat.
- Aries Stadium becomes a primary locus of investigation.
