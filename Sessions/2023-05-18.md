# Session 2023-05-18

## Summary

The crew continued the toxic egg / magical biomaterial investigation, learned more about the eggshells' draconic and toxic nature, and traced the clue chain through street contacts and an old man with a shrine to the mayor.

## Major Scenes

- Crew regrouped at Atlas's and reviewed the eggshell fragments.
- Kari Wilkerson briefed them further on the magical danger and urgency.
- Mevin and Glenn pursued leads in the streets and discovered they were being watched by a cloaked drone.
- Mevin recovered eggshell fragments from a deranged old man's hut and extracted a rough geographic lead south of town.

## NPCs Introduced / In Play

- Kari Wilkerson
- Samuel Jones
- unnamed old man with shrine to the mayor

## Clues Gained

- eggshells appeared draconic and toxic
- the material was part of a much larger egg
- a cloaked/ruthenium drone was surveilling the crew
- the old man claimed he found the original giant egg in the woods during rural evacuation work

## Decisions Made

- continue the hunt for the source/nest
- preserve and contain the recovered biomaterial

## Changes to Campaign State

- the investigation shifted from mere contraband recovery toward a possible dragon-egg/nest mystery with political and magical implications

## Open Threads

- where the nest actually is
- who is surveilling the crew with the cloaked drone
- how the mayoral symbolism around the old man fits into the larger truth
