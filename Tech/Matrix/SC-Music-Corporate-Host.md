# SC Music Corporate Host

## Overview

A corporate media host tied to SC Music's artist management, promotional scheduling, distribution planning, branding assets, contracts, and behavior-shaping market analysis.

## What deckers find inside

- artist calendars, release plans, and tour-support logistics
- contract libraries, royalty structures, and executive communications
- market research, audience segmentation, and promo campaign analytics
- media asset vaults including masters, ad packages, and approved image kits
- restricted strategy spaces where talent handling and influence operations blur together

## Host feel

This host is glossy, seductive, and manipulative by design. It presents as a fusion of studio lot, executive lounge, digital stage set, and controlled dream-factory.

The sculpting flatters the intruder before it judges them.

## Iconography

- user-facing directories appear as **gold record walls, marquee boards, and green-room mirrors**
- archived media appears as **master tapes, crystal reels, and locked dressing trunks**
- IC manifests as **security staff, handlers, stage managers, and smiling promo avatars with dead eyes**
- alert states shift the environment from glamorous spotlight to hostile backstage lockdown
- high-value hidden files may appear as unreleased tracks sealed in velvet-lined vault rooms

## Decker use

Useful for tracing contracts, sponsorship webs, publicity manipulation, internal rivalries, blackmail material, and the machinery behind who gets made or broken in Nashville's entertainment scene.
