# Krom and the Brotherhood of Evil Mutants

## Overview

A historical high-concept side-thread in which Krom is kidnapped by Sebastian and held in a lab-like basement environment tied to the Brotherhood of Evil Mutants.

## What the source preserves

- Krom was kidnapped by Sebastian.
- At least two Brotherhood members were involved, including Quicksilver (already dead by the source note) and another medically-oriented figure Krom only heard.
- Krom was being held in a 20x10 meter basement.
- Half the basement was cleared; the other half resembled a lab with medical equipment.
- Krom's gear was stored at the bottom of the stairs.

## Why this page exists

The surviving note is vivid enough to preserve, but too isolated to pretend it is currently a major campaign spine.
