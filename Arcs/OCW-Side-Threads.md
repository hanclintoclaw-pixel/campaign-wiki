# OCW Side Threads

## Overview

A small historical side-thread bucket tied to OCW match fallout, Owl-man merchandising, and the possibility of turning wrestling-show momentum into contracts, appearances, and negotiation leverage.

## What the source preserves

- Krom was owed a small but notable payout tied to a house take after **New Jack's** loss.
- A bookie named **Kowalski** wanted Krom to attend the next match and collect his share.
- **Owl-man** merchandise was selling extremely well.
- There was explicit room to negotiate a more formal OCW arrangement around appearance fees, creative control, title reign terms, and merch participation.

## Why this page exists

The Wordpress note reads more like sidequest / campaign-management scaffolding than a major core arc, so this page preserves the material without pretending it had the same weight as the main historical threads.
