# Mucky and Professor X

## Overview

A historical side-thread focused less on action and more on identity: Mucky seeking out Professor X to understand where he came from, what kind of being he has always been, and whether his personhood has changed over time.

## What the source preserves

- Between missions, Mucky sought an audience with Professor X.
- The questions he wanted answered were deeply personal:
  - where he came from
  - whether he had always been able to be a person
  - whether he had been other people before
- The note implies this was a potentially serious character-development thread rather than simple exposition.
