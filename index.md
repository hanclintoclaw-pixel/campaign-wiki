# NASHVILLE SHADOWRUN

*Chrome, country, corporate lies, and the smell of ozone in the rain.*

Welcome to the shared campaign memory for the **Nashville Shadowrun** game.

This wiki is meant to serve three jobs at once:
- a **player-facing campaign reference**
- a **GM-usable continuity tool**
- a **living memory system** maintained by Cindy Lou and reviewed by the table

## Current Situation

The crew is investigating a hostile influence network moving through Nashville's music and media layers.
Recent pressure points include:
- **Darla Ledue**
- **SC Music**
- a suspicious **anonymous sponsor / agent**
- an upscale **corporate-owned penthouse / recording studio**
- a monitored **encrypted satellite contact path**
