# Gladwell Institute

## Overview

A rehab facility serving high-profile clients. In the recovered archive, it sits at the center of a leak investigation involving security, medical suspicion, and possible internal manipulation.

## What the crew knows

- Gladwell served famous or politically sensitive clients.
- Embarrassing substance-abuse stories about prominent figures were leaking out.
- The leak case touched both security and medical personnel.
- The internal picture did not look clean or simple; **Eugene Booker** may have been frightened, compromised, or set up.
