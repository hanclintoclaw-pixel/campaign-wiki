# Earl "Railgun" Dunlap

## Overview

**Earl "Railgun" Dunlap** is a rail-and-river logistics decker tied to the [Lookout Mountain Collective](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md). He understands freight movement, maintenance windows, and the way physical systems betray digital lies.

## Known Facts

- Uses the handle **Railgun**.
- Watches rail, freight, river, and municipal logistics around Chattanooga.
- Treats schedules as evidence and delays as confessions.
- Has a practical streak: if the data says a truck was in two places at once, he wants to know who signed the lie.

## Role in the Campaign

- Useful contact for freight trails, route timing, utility contractors, and physical-world validation of Matrix records.
- Can help make Ruby Falls feel grounded in Chattanooga's real logistics rather than abstract server space.
- Potential bridge between Mevin's Matrix work and Curtis-style vehicle/logistics thinking.

## Personality

Railgun is cheerful, blunt, and allergic to mystique. He calls complicated data fraud "bad bookkeeping with a hat on." He likes trains, hates sloppy timestamps, and thinks half of Matrix security is just logistics wearing sunglasses.

## Relationships

- [Lookout Mountain Collective](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md) - logistics watcher and decker.
- [Mevin Kitnick](../PCs/Mevin-Kitnick.md) - possible data-routing or freight-record contact.
- [Curtis](../PCs/Curtis.md) - not yet connected, but their logistics/mechanics interests could overlap.

## Capabilities / Resources

- Freight, rail, river, and municipal route analysis.
- Timestamp reconciliation and physical-world plausibility checks.
- Access to local chatter among maintenance workers, drivers, and rail watchers.
- Strong instincts for whether a Matrix record matches real movement.
