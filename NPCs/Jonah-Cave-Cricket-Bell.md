# Jonah "Cave Cricket" Bell

## Overview

**Jonah "Cave Cricket" Bell** is a quiet sysop and sensor obsessive attached to the [Lookout Mountain Collective](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md). He is the sort of decker who notices a route is wrong because the latency sounds different.

## Known Facts

- Uses the handle **Cave Cricket**.
- Works around Ruby Falls infrastructure and monitoring culture.
- Has a reputation for saying very little, then pointing at the one line in a log that matters.
- Likes signal-noise puzzles, echo patterns, and weird sensor correlations.

## Role in the Campaign

- Good contact for uptime, intrusion traces, signal anomalies, and "something moved in the dark" Matrix clues.
- Can serve as the Collective's technical worrywart when Ruby Falls or Chattanooga infrastructure feels pressured.
- Useful for making data-haven security feel alive without revealing the Host itself yet.

## Personality

Cave Cricket speaks softly and often through clipped observations: "That echo's wrong," "same packet, different cave," or "somebody left mud on the route." He is not shy; he just thinks most sentences are too long.

## Relationships

- [Lookout Mountain Collective](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md) - sysop-adjacent monitor and infrastructure watcher.
- [Maribel "Switchback" Pruitt](Maribel-Switchback-Pruitt.md) - likely works with her on safe routes and anomaly checks.
- [Mevin Kitnick](../PCs/Mevin-Kitnick.md) - may know him as a quiet Ruby Falls presence.

## Capabilities / Resources

- Signal monitoring and anomaly detection.
- Sensor-log correlation, especially when physical infrastructure and Matrix routes overlap.
- Quiet maintenance of alerting, tripwire, and status systems.
- Local knowledge of caves, tourist systems, and Chattanooga signal oddities.
