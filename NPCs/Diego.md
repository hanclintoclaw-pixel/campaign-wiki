# Diego

## Overview

Hard-looking ganger / possible cybersam who accompanied **Alvin Flang** during the observed **Music Circle** meeting.

## Known Facts

- Diego was identified by name only because **Mevin** successfully isolated and listened to the park-bench conversation.
- He appeared to be acting as **Alvin Flang's protection** or escort during the meet.
- He wore iconography associated with **[Heavenly Host](../Factions/Heavenly-Host.md)**.
- Kurgan read him as a visibly dangerous street operator rather than an office functionary.
- Diego spent the meeting hyping Alvin up and reinforcing Alvin's sense that they were doing well.
- He was the only one in Alvin's group who seemed at all likely to notice Kurgan moving closer.

## Relationships

- Linked to **[Alvin Flang](Alvin-Flang.md)** as an observed companion/bodyguard.
- Linked to **[Heavenly Host](../Factions/Heavenly-Host.md)** through visible gang colors/iconography.
- Potentially linked to the still-unidentified buyer network behind the Griswell data leak.
