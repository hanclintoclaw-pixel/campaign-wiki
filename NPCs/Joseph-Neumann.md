# Joseph Neumann

## Overview

City power-broker figure and mayoral intermediary. By the 2066-05-09 interlude, Joseph was acting as **Mayor Mike Haggar's** chief-of-staff / ombudsman figure and directly routed deniable work to **Kurgan**.

## Known Facts

- Joseph Neumann is explicitly named alongside Herrick in the city-power-broker / debrief posture after the anti-insect-spirit strike phase.
- Joseph maintained a working relationship with Kurgan after the insect-spirit mayoral operation.
- Joseph contacted Kurgan with an opportunity to solve the **Righteous Haze** problem for the mayor.
- Joseph handled the logistics that let Kurgan and Curtis cancel / reroute Haysen's normal pickup from **Josie's Billiard Hall**.
- After Haysen was removed, Joseph told Kurgan that the mayor looked forward to further developing the relationship.

## Relationships

- Linked to **Herrick** in the containment/debrief posture.
- Linked to the crew through the aftermath of major city-level operations.
- Linked to **[Mayor Mike Haggar](Mayor-Mike-Haggar.md)** as a mayoral staff / intermediary figure.
- Linked to **[Kurgan](../PCs/Kurgan.md)** through the Righteous Haze interlude.

## Relevant Sessions

- 2026-03-19 — active elimination transitioned into containment/debrief with city power brokers.
- 2026-06-25 — contacted Kurgan on the mayor's behalf and handled pickup logistics during the Righteous Haze interlude.
