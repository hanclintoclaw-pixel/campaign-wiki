# Leona "Auntie Static" Voss

## Overview

**Leona "Auntie Static" Voss** is an older signal tinkerer, pirate-radio veteran, and back-room organizer attached to [Noodle Stories](../Locations/Noodle-Stories.md). She treats poetry, radio, and rumor as different ways of keeping people from disappearing quietly.

## Known Facts

- Goes by **Auntie Static** around Noodle Stories.
- Old enough to remember earlier crackdowns and petty enough to remember individual badge numbers.
- Helps the [Noodle Stories Collective](../Organizations/Noodle-Stories-Collective.md) keep communications low-tech, deniable, and hard to scrape.
- Has a dry, affectionate manner with regulars and a freezing stare for anyone who asks too many operational questions too quickly.
- Keeps a battered radio kit and tool roll in the back room, though nobody admits it is a station.

## Role in the Campaign

- Potential contact for analog signals, rumor verification, and arts-district warning networks.
- Good NPC for explaining why the collective distrusts convenient Matrix tools.
- Can provide old protest history, local pattern recognition, and a bridge between poets and practical security.

## Relationships

- [Noodle Stories](../Locations/Noodle-Stories.md) - back-room regular and signal minder.
- [Noodle Stories Collective](../Organizations/Noodle-Stories-Collective.md) - elder organizer and communications conscience.
- [Mina "Red Silk" Park](Mina-Red-Silk-Park.md) - trusted by Mina with keys, warnings, and hard truths.
- [Otis "Gutter Sonnet" Bell](Otis-Gutter-Sonnet-Bell.md) - indulges his performances when they are useful and heckles them when they are not.

## Capabilities / Resources

- Pirate-radio habits, analog message discipline, and practical electronics repair.
- Knowledge of older Nashville protest networks and arts-district pressure history.
- Ability to move warnings without leaving a clean Matrix trail.
- Social authority with regulars who treat her as everyone's aunt and no one's fool.
