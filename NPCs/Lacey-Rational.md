# Lacey Rational

## Overview

Leader of the Ironblood Orphans and one of the principal figures in the 2023-03-23 gang peace-meeting run.

## Known Facts

- Lacey Rational leads the **Ironblood Orphans**.
- She came into the peace-meeting negotiations with a structured territorial/business proposal.
- She appeared heavily cybered and extremely fast, to the point of being compared favorably against Kurgan's speed.
- She was willing to carry a long gun and was confident in her ability to kill opponents with cyber claws if things went bad.
- Mevin judged her business proposal surprisingly reasonable, even while remaining suspicious that she was hiding something.

## Relationships

- Leader of the **Ironblood Orphans**.
- Opposed to / negotiating with the **Interstate Connection**.
- Interacted directly with the crew during negotiation-security prep.
