# Professor "Chuck" X

## Overview

A powerful magical educator and patron figure operating a school for gifted youngsters outside Nashville, with a specific tie to Mucky.

## Known Facts

- Professor "Chuck" X is human.
- He is described as a wheelchair-using, bald, somewhat older schoolmaster.
- He runs the **School for Gifted Youngsters** outside Nashville.
- The school trains younger people who are gifted magically or otherwise.
- He possesses magical ability far beyond what is normally seen even among elite shadowrun magicians.
- He has an idealistic worldview, but is willing to accept harsh measures in dangerous times.
- He has been scouting for an **X-Force** of cutthroat mercenaries to keep his school safe.
- He runs two initiate groups: one for casters and one for adepts.
- He has significant money from unknown origins.
- Historical note material shows **Mucky** seeking him out for deeper identity/personal-history questions.

## Relationships

- Linked to [Mucky](Mucky.md).
- Linked to the School for Gifted Youngsters.
