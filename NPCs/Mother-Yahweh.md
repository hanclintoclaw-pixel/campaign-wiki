# Mother Yahweh

## Overview

Cultish figure tied to the counterfeit-food / insect-burger arc.

## Known Facts

- Mother Yahweh interviewed Kurgan and Generic during the wildcat Stuffer Shack investigation.
- She attempted to whammy Generic and seemed to reassess him when it failed.
- Her presence reinforced that the operation had overt cult-like components beyond mere counterfeit commerce.
- By **2026-05-21**, she was physically confirmed at the **612 Wharf Avenue** warehouse layer of the modern Darla/Bebop counterfeit-goods operation.
- That warehouse was processing counterfeit **Meatlets** using material cut from a chained **roach spirit**, tying Mother Yahweh directly to the magical contamination pipeline.
- Session evidence suggests she and **Core 7 / Site 7** were at minimum operationally linked, even if the exact command relationship remains unclear.

## Relationships

- Linked to the counterfeit-food / insect-spirit burger arc.
- Directly linked to the **612 Wharf Avenue** warehouse processing layer.
- Potentially linked to the broader **Core 7 / Site 7** influence-control structure.

## Relevant Sessions

- 2023-12-21 — cult/insect-material escalation in the counterfeit Stuffer Shack arc.
- 2026-05-21 — physically present at the 612 Wharf warehouse; tied to the spirit-harvesting counterfeit Meatlets line.

## Open Questions

- Was Mother Yahweh a true leader, recruiter, or just a charismatic node inside a larger structure?
- Was she subordinate to **Core 7**, manipulating it, or simply sharing adjacent infrastructure?
- How much of the warehouse-side spirit-harvesting operation depended specifically on her?
