# Kari Wilkerson

## Overview

Senior Draco Foundation representative who hired the crew into the toxic-egg / magical-artifact hunt.

## Known Facts

- Kari Wilkerson is a very senior member of the **Draco Foundation**.
- She met the crew openly through Ivan rather than behind a standard anonymized Johnson layer.
- She sought recovery of highly dangerous anomalous biological material before it could be sold or mishandled.
- She came across as professional, urgent, and legitimately concerned.
- Glenn read her as something like a do-gooder civil-rights lawyer; she is also identified as a hermetic mage carrying at least one weapon focus.

## Relationships

- Linked to the **Draco Foundation**.
- Linked to **Ivan Toklas** through the job setup.
- Opposed to irresponsible handling of toxic magical biomaterial.

## Relevant Sessions

- 2023-05-11 — first job briefing.
- 2023-05-18 — continued egg/toxic-material investigation.
