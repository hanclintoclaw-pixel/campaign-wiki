# Dr. Bruce Gallstone

## Overview

Mevin-linked veterinarian, awakened-biology contact, and discreet animal-medicine source. He was previously split in the wiki as "Dr. Gallstone" and "Bruce Gallstone"; player correction on 2026-07-10 confirmed these are the same NPC.

## Known Facts

- Bruce is the veterinarian contact the crew had leaned on before, usually referred to as Dr. Gallstone in older notes.
- Mevin remembers him as an old college buddy in veterinary medicine and a discreet source for animal medicines such as tranquilizers, stimulants, and creature-handling advice.
- He has discussed unusual biological/magical matters with the crew, including dragon tissue, unicorns, and sasquatches.
- Earlier notes say he has a phone number for awakened-animal cases and does not handle them directly because treating awakened animals in a normal animal clinic is illegal.
- Player recollection on 2026-07-10 says Bruce previously helped during the rescue of a sasquatch from an underground fighting ring by connecting the crew with awakened-animal-rights transport support; treat the exact organization/name as unconfirmed until a source note surfaces.
- During the post-federal unrest, he called in a favor and asked the crew to help protect his veterinary practice.
- His clinic sat inside an arcoblock vulnerable to riot spillover and property damage.
- Bruce met the crew in the lobby, escorted them through the arcoblock, and helped orient them to the building's social terrain.
- He suggested where troublemakers could be displaced and later confirmed that the crew's first night of crowd control had kept property damage lower than feared.

## Relationships

- Linked to the federal unrest / Gallstone favor arc.
