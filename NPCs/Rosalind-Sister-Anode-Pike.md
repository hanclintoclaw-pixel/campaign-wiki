# Rosalind "Sister Anode" Pike

## Overview

**Rosalind "Sister Anode" Pike** is an archivist and data-ethics hardliner within the [Lookout Mountain Collective](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md). She treats preserved data like testimony: useful, dangerous, and morally weighty.

## Known Facts

- Uses the handle **Sister Anode**.
- Associated with Ruby Falls archival standards and access etiquette.
- Cares deeply about source protection, consent, and whether leaked data will hurt the wrong people.
- Has enough moral gravity that even noisy deckers tend to lower their volume around her.

## Role in the Campaign

- Strong contact for deciding whether dangerous paydata should be preserved, shared, redacted, or quarantined.
- Can challenge Mevin or the crew when they bring in information that is powerful but ethically messy.
- Good voice for Ruby Falls as a haven, not merely a storage Host.

## Personality

Sister Anode is kind without being soft. She has the cadence of a church auntie, a librarian, and a circuit tester sharing one chair. She will feed you soup, encrypt your testimony, and ask exactly who gets hurt if you publish.

## Relationships

- [Lookout Mountain Collective](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md) - archivist and conscience figure.
- [Ruby Falls](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md#resources) - associated with preservation rules and source-safety culture.
- [Mevin Kitnick](../PCs/Mevin-Kitnick.md) - may become a source-handling mentor or stern reviewer.

## Capabilities / Resources

- Data curation, redaction, and archive integrity.
- Whistleblower intake practices.
- Community trust among people who fear institutions more than criminals.
- A sharp sense for when a leak is bait.
