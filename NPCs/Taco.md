# Taco

![Taco](../assets/images/Taco.png)

## Overview

Recurring NPC contact who runs a large vehicle repair/salvage operation and is especially important to Curtis.

## Known Facts

- Taco is an NPC and ex-PC.
- He runs **[Taco's Garage and Workshop](../Locations/Tacos-Garage-and-Workshop.md)**, a large vehicle repair / salvage operation at **216-226 Thompson Ln, Nashville, TN 37211**.
- He is an active contact for the group.
- Curtis is explicitly noted as an ally and student of Taco.
- By **2026-05-28**, Taco's property was being considered as possible support infrastructure for a future **Core 7** dolphin relocation/rescue plan.
- As of 2026-07-03, Taco's site is the mapped location for **Curtis's Dolphin Rescue**.

## Relationships

- Linked strongly to **Curtis** as mentor/ally.
- Connected to the crew through logistics, vehicles, and repair/salvage support.
- Connected to the **Core 7 / Site 7** fallout through Curtis's effort to prepare a possible dolphin habitat/landing space at [Taco's Garage and Workshop](../Locations/Tacos-Garage-and-Workshop.md).
