# Righteous Haze

## Overview

**Righteous Haze**, real name **Darrell Haysen**, was an older-generation Nashville shadowrunner / fixer who had spent years as an outside security consultant and dossier-preparer for **Mayor Mike Haggar**. By 2066, he had become complacent, corrupt, and dangerous enough that the mayor hired **Kurgan** and **Curtis** to remove him.

## Known Facts

- Kurgan recognized Righteous Haze as an old Nashville underworld name from his younger Humanis-era days.
- Haze was understood as a shadow operator from an earlier generation who had moved toward retirement by becoming a fixer/security consultant.
- For years, he prepared dossiers and helped arrange outside operatives for the mayor's deniable work.
- He had been taking large payments while providing little useful current work.
- He had a substance-abuse problem, a gambling habit, and a pattern of drinking at **[Josie's Billiard Hall](../Locations/Josies-Billiard-Hall.md)**.
- He acted like a petty loan shark at Josie's, fronting gambling money at catastrophic rates.
- He threatened to expose years of dirt on the mayor and city operations when the mayor tried to remove him.
- His cyberware was largely outdated or nonfunctional by the time Kurgan and Curtis killed him.
- He possessed a data bank containing records of jobs he had pulled for the mayor and other senior city officials.
- Kurgan and Curtis killed him during the 2066-05-09 interlude and left his distinctive cyberhand nailed to Josie's door.

## Relationships

- Formerly linked to **[Mayor Mike Haggar](Mayor-Mike-Haggar.md)** as a deniable fixer/security consultant.
- Removed by **[Kurgan](../PCs/Kurgan.md)** and **[Curtis](../PCs/Curtis.md)** at Haggar's request.
- Routed through **[Joseph Neumann](Joseph-Neumann.md)** as the mayoral intermediary.
- Associated with **[Josie's Billiard Hall](../Locations/Josies-Billiard-Hall.md)** as his regular hangout and local power base.
