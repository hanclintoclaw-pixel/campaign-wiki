# Merle Graham

## Overview

A high-profile Nashville entertainment-industry figure: record producer, manipulator, drunk, and fixture of the city's gossip-rich upper layers.

## Known Facts

- Merle Graham is a **record producer**.
- He is also described as a semi-professional alcoholic and pimp.
- He is characterized as a booze-addled maniac with a heart of coal.
- He helped launch **Hank 4's** last three major comebacks.
- He has taken young **Hank 5** under his wing.
- He owns the top three floors of the newly renovated **Hank 4 Tower**.
- Rumors about his penthouse club, **Cannonade**, are a major feature of local gossip.

## Relationships
