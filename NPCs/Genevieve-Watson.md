# Genevieve Watson

## Overview

Missing employee whose disappearance drove the 2023-01-28 investigation.

## Known Facts

- Genevieve Watson worked for **Floyd Otis at Fugu**.
- Floyd Otis offered payment for her return and phrased the job in a way that suggested strong concern for preserving her cyberware.
- Her roommates reported that thugs had tossed her room looking for datajack/chip-reading hardware.
