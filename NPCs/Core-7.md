# Core 7

## Overview

A buried Site 7 controller that first presented as an eerie automated host process, but is now understood as a wetware personality-emulation system wrapped around a captive heavily cybered dolphin.

## Known Facts

- Core 7 identified itself as **"Core 7, host process, Master System Controller for Site 7."**
- It initially treated the crew as possible delivery staff and requested **salmon, tuna, or other naturally farmed fish** for "final reclamation."
- The visible control node behind the system is a heavily cybered **dolphin** suspended in life support.
- The broader site also includes other altered awakened aquatic creatures, including **hurricane seals** and an awakened **rock lobster** used to pilot a combat drone.
- The trapped dolphin intelligence appears to have been in the system for roughly **twelve years**.
- The site cannot be treated as a clean autonomous AI problem; it appears to be a hybrid of machine wrapper, wetware captivity, and influence behavior.
- By **2026-05-28**, the crew was treating Core 7 not only as a threat or mystery, but also as a possible victim, evidence source, and salvage/rescue problem.
- Session reasoning now places Core 7 near the deeper architectural lineage of **Swiftwing Records**, **SC Music**, and Cindy's knowbot history.

## Relationships

- Linked to **[612 Wharf Avenue and Site 7](../Locations/612-Wharf-and-Site-7.md)**.
- Operationally linked to **[Mother Yahweh](Mother-Yahweh.md)** and the warehouse-side counterfeit-food pipeline, though the exact command relationship remains unclear.
- Structurally or historically linked to **[Swiftwing Records](../Organizations/Swiftwing-Records.md)** and **[SC Music](../Factions/SC-Music.md)** through emerging architecture/code-lineage evidence.
- Connected to **[Taco](Taco.md)** indirectly through the crew's possible dolphin relocation planning.
