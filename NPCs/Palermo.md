# Palermo

## Overview

**Palermo** is a Nashville-market paydata broker identified as the downstream buyer/source behind **Alvin Flang's** suspicious data activity at **Griswell Data Services**.

## Known Facts

- Palermo is known as a broker of paydata and corporate information.
- He maintains Matrix-facing contact through the **Ruby Falls data haven** in Chattanooga.
- His listings specifically cover corporate information in and around the Nashville market.
- **Kurgan** recognized the name from old **Humanis**-adjacent business, where higher-ups had used Palermo or his network for information work.
- Palermo appears to find and exploit weak links inside companies rather than always targeting a company for personal reasons.
- In the Griswell case, he treated **Alvin Flang** as an exploitable vulnerability.
- He operated from a protected structure in the [Music Circle Underground Market](../Locations/Music-Circle-Underground-Market.md), supported by hidden power and Matrix utility connections.
- When confronted by the crew, he gave up a burner identity sufficient to satisfy the Griswell contract.
- He is likely to burn compromised locations after exposure.

## Relationships

- Linked to **[Alvin Flang](Alvin-Flang.md)** as the broker exploiting Alvin's access.
- Linked to **[Griswell Data Services](../Organizations/Griswell-Data-Services.md)** through the concluded data-leak contract.
- Linked to **[Music Circle Underground Market](../Locations/Music-Circle-Underground-Market.md)** as an operating location / safehouse.
- Potential future contact for **[Mevin Kitnick](../PCs/Mevin-Kitnick.md)** if cultivated carefully.
- Historically familiar to **[Kurgan](../PCs/Kurgan.md)** through old Humanis-linked information work.
