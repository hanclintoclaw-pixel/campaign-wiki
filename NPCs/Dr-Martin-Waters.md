# Dr. Martin Waters

## Overview

Medical figure who appears in both the Gladwell leak investigation and the Genevieve Watson cyberware trail.

## Known Facts

- At Gladwell, Dr. Martin Waters was a relatively recent awakened hire and drew suspicion because Glenn believed he might be using magic to detoxify people.
- In the Genevieve investigation, **Dr. Waters** was the medical contact Floyd Otis eventually named after lying about records.
- The surviving archive leaves some ambiguity about whether these references are certainly the same person, but they are similar enough to flag as likely connected pending stronger evidence.

## Relationships

- Linked to **Gladwell Institute**.
- Linked to **Floyd Otis** and **Genevieve Watson**.

## Relevant Sessions

- 2022-08-11 — Gladwell leak investigation.
- 2023-01-28 — Genevieve Watson investigation.

## Open Questions

- Are the Gladwell Dr. Martin Waters and Genevieve's Dr. Waters definitely the same person?
- If so, how far does his involvement in coercive medical/cyberware activity extend?
