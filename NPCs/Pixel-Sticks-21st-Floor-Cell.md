# Pixel Sticks 21st-Floor Cell

## Overview

The **Pixel Sticks 21st-floor cell** was the physical crew operating from the upper floors of the [Vernon Winfrey Arkoblock](../Locations/Vernon-Winfrey-Arkoblock.md). The cell had controlled access, cameras, decker hardware, host equipment tied to the [Happy Cat Public Storefront Host](../Tech/Matrix/Happy-Cat-Public-Storefront-Host.md), and at least one extremely dangerous scorpion-like combat drone.

The crew disrupted the cell in [Session 2026-07-23](../Sessions/2026-07-23.md), recovered used utilities for the Johnson-side handoff, and left the floor in chaos while the drone fought [Chunky Sparkles](Chunky-Sparkles.md).

## Known Members / Handles

- **Highbrow** - decker/leader voice encountered through an orc-barbarian Matrix icon on local display hardware; rejected Mevin's warning and threatened to have the crew thrown out.
- **Handy-Sandy** - handle recovered from the member-list / route-board context and used as bluff material.
- **Pop Onomata** - handle recovered from the member-list / route-board context and used as bluff material.
- **Ernie Pollock** - name present on the scorpion drone's whitelist.
- **Ziggy Hill** - name present on the scorpion drone's whitelist.
- **Lizzo Wells** - young woman associated with Happy Cat and present on the scorpion drone's whitelist; arrived with Chunky Sparkles during the crew's escape.
- **Unnamed guards / goons** - physical door security; one was killed by Valgaut during the breach.
- **Sickly elf decker** - found lying in or near the equipment area, status unresolved in the public notes.

## Capabilities / Resources

- Controlled 21st-floor access point with guards.
- Camera coverage and display hardware for Matrix-icon intimidation / remote speaking.
- Host terminal and Matrix infrastructure connected to the Happy Cat laser-array path.
- Used military-grade utilities recovered by the crew.
- Additional encrypted utility/paydata material retained by Mevin for later research.
- Access to or operational contact with the [Pixel Sticks Scorpion Drone](../Vehicles/Pixel-Sticks-Scorpion-Drone.md).

## Current Status

Disrupted. The crew breached the floor, disabled or intimidated several members, recovered used utilities, and triggered the scorpion drone as escape-cover. Survivors, sponsors, and the cell's ability to rebuild remain unknown.
