# Maribel "Switchback" Pruitt

## Overview

**Maribel "Switchback" Pruitt** is a Chattanooga decker and route-mapper in the [Lookout Mountain Collective](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md). She is one of the people most likely to decide whether an outsider is using Ruby Falls responsibly or treating it like a free vending machine for secrets.

## Known Facts

- Uses the handle **Switchback**.
- Associated with the Lookout Mountain Collective and the Ruby Falls data haven.
- Specializes in safe routes, provenance chains, and knowing when a clean path is too clean.
- Has a reputation for being polite right up until she is not.

## Role in the Campaign

- Potential contact or gatekeeper for Mevin's Ruby Falls access.
- Useful for route hygiene, source validation, and deciding whether a data handoff smells staged.
- Good face for the Collective when the table needs one person to talk to instead of an entire chorus of suspicious deckers.

## Personality

Switchback is warm, precise, and quietly unforgiving. She talks like someone giving hiking directions to a person she suspects may ignore the part about loose gravel. If she likes you, she gives warnings early. If she does not, she lets the terrain teach.

## Relationships

- [Lookout Mountain Collective](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md) - trusted member and route-minded organizer.
- [Mevin Kitnick](../PCs/Mevin-Kitnick.md) - likely contact point or known-access reviewer; exact relationship TBD.
- [Ruby Falls](../Factions/Lookout-Mountain-Collective.md#resources) - helps protect access discipline around the data haven.

## Capabilities / Resources

- Matrix route mapping and safe-access planning.
- Source-vetting and data provenance discipline.
- Regional knowledge of Chattanooga grid geography, tourist systems, and utility routing.
- Enough social capital inside the Collective to slow or smooth access for outsiders.
