# Mina "Red Silk" Park

## Overview

**Mina "Red Silk" Park** owns and runs [Noodle Stories](../Locations/Noodle-Stories.md), keeping the broth hot, the poets fed, and the back room just orderly enough that the [Noodle Stories Collective](../Organizations/Noodle-Stories-Collective.md) can survive in Nashville's arts district.

## Known Facts

- Uses the street/house name **Red Silk**.
- Runs the shop with a practical mix of hospitality, suspicion, and bookkeeping.
- Feeds hungry regulars even when the ledger says she should not.
- Has a reputation for remembering who paid late, who lied badly, and who protected someone weaker.
- Keeps the collective grounded when its poets drift toward reckless spectacle.

## Role in the Campaign

- Potential neighborhood contact for arts-district rumors, low-profile meetings, or mutual-aid introductions.
- Gatekeeper for access to the shop's back room and the collective's trust.
- Useful face for scenes where food, hospitality, and politics all matter at once.

## Relationships

- [Noodle Stories](../Locations/Noodle-Stories.md) - owner/operator.
- [Noodle Stories Collective](../Organizations/Noodle-Stories-Collective.md) - host, protector, and practical anchor.
- [Otis "Gutter Sonnet" Bell](Otis-Gutter-Sonnet-Bell.md) - valued but exhausting house poet.
- [Leona "Auntie Static" Voss](Leona-Auntie-Static-Voss.md) - trusted elder and back-room problem solver.

## Capabilities / Resources

- Neighborhood social intelligence from customers, suppliers, artists, and late-night regulars.
- Kitchen, back-room, and service-corridor access at Noodle Stories.
- Strong memory for faces, debts, favors, and pressure patterns.
- Enough local goodwill to make harassment of the shop politically noisy.
