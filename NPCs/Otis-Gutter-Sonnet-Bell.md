# Otis "Gutter Sonnet" Bell

## Overview

**Otis "Gutter Sonnet" Bell** is the house poet and informal master of ceremonies at [Noodle Stories](../Locations/Noodle-Stories.md), where his jokes are too sharp, his politics are too loud, and his metaphors sometimes carry real neighborhood warnings.

## Known Facts

- Performs under the name **Gutter Sonnet**.
- Hosts late-night readings for the [Noodle Stories Collective](../Organizations/Noodle-Stories-Collective.md).
- Specializes in funny, ugly, anti-authoritarian poems that sound half like confession and half like threat assessment.
- Knows the arts-district gossip web because everyone assumes poets are broke, harmless, and listening only to themselves.
- Can be charming, irritating, or both inside the same sentence.

## Role in the Campaign

- Public-facing voice of Noodle Stories when a scene needs texture, warning, or coded performance.
- Potential source for street-level arts-district rumor and protest-scene mood.
- Good delivery mechanism for clues hidden in a poem, flyer, open-mic introduction, or heckled aside.

## Relationships

- [Noodle Stories](../Locations/Noodle-Stories.md) - regular performer and late-night fixture.
- [Noodle Stories Collective](../Organizations/Noodle-Stories-Collective.md) - poet, host, and agitator.
- [Mina "Red Silk" Park](Mina-Red-Silk-Park.md) - respects her authority, usually after testing it.
- [Leona "Auntie Static" Voss](Leona-Auntie-Static-Voss.md) - swaps old protest references and signal jokes with her.

## Capabilities / Resources

- Social access to poets, musicians, gallery workers, venue staff, and broke students.
- Skilled public performer who can distract a room or focus it instantly.
- Knows how to phrase dangerous facts as art, gossip, or plausible nonsense.
- Has enough pride to be manipulated by praise and enough conscience to regret it later.
