# Imogene Viola

## Overview

Cross Applied Technology executive who hired the crew to recover stolen DNA sequencing and cloning equipment.

## Known Facts

- Imogene Viola presented herself as a senior director in forward agrarian strategy for Cross Applied Technology's CAS division.
- She showed the crew security footage of a clumsy imitation team stealing corporate equipment.
- She wanted the stolen hardware recovered and was prepared to bury the false accusation against the crew if they took the job.
- The gear in question involved high-end DNA sequencing and cloning technology.

## Relationships

- Linked to **Cross Applied Technology**.
- Linked to **6 Steel Strings** indirectly through the theft/impersonation aftermath.
