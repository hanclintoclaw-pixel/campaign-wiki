# Friendly Phyllis

## Overview

Nashville fence who traditionally holds court in a corner booth at Atlas's.

## Known Facts

- Friendly Phyllis is a troll.
- She works as a fence.
- She is described as a hulking brute of a woman who is surprisingly friendly.
- She will receive stolen goods from just about anyone.
- She also offers information on deals for the right price.
- She reportedly keeps liquid assets of over a million nuyen.
- She drives a hard bargain.
- Historical stats note Negotiation 6, INT 3, and WIL 3.

## Relationships

- Strongly linked to **Atlas's**.
