# Lorenzo Zoyle

## Overview

A criminal underworld figure with roots in the LA syndicate scene who reinvented himself in Nashville as a BTL dealer, part-time DJ, and occasional avatar for **Poisonous Justice**.

## Known Facts

- Lorenzo was formerly a low-ranking member of an LA-area criminal syndicate.
- He was orphaned when the Pacific Rim Quakes destroyed most of southern California.
- He now works as a **BTL dealer**.
- He also works part-time as a **DJ**.
- He is occasionally pressed into service as the physical avatar of **Poisonous Justice**.

## Relationships

- Linked to BTL/criminal subcultures.
- Linked to **Poisonous Justice**.
