# March Hare

## Overview

Commander of the Interstate Connection and one of the two gang leaders at the center of the 2023-03-23 peace-meeting run.

## Known Facts

- March Hare is the commander of the **Interstate Connection**.
- She is described as a very large troll wearing security armor and carrying a very large long rifle.
- She intended to arrive at the peace meeting with two vehicles carrying eight total people.
- She insisted on keeping close security, but accepted compromises so long as line of sight to her people was maintained.

## Relationships

- Leader of the **Interstate Connection**.
- Negotiating counterpart to **Lacey Rational** of the Ironblood Orphans.
- Directly negotiated logistical/security terms with the crew.

## Relevant Sessions

- 2023-03-23 — peace-meeting preparation between rival gun-running gangs.
