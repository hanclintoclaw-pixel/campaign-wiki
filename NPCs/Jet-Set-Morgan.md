# Jet Set Morgan

## Overview

Active surveillance target in the April 9 session.

## Known Facts

- The crew chose to tail Jet Set Morgan rather than grab him immediately.
- Kurgan followed him on foot while Curtis supported with drone surveillance.
- Another unknown drone also appeared to be following him.
- He moved through public streets and into alleyways, increasing the risk profile of the tail.
- After capture, he was revealed to be a gig-economy stooge working for the same employer behind the street-promoter / Darla-distribution side of the operation.
- His van contained a second satellite receiver, and his cyberterminal was root-kitted in a way that let a controller exert deep influence over his life.
- The crew ultimately released him and treated him as a potential **Level 1 contact for Kurgan** rather than a pure disposable target.

## Relationships

- Linked to the crew as a current operational target.
- Linked to the unknown second drone / unknown watcher.
- Linked to the broader Darla / counterfeit-distribution / meat-drone network.
- Linked to Kurgan as a possible low-level future contact after his release.

## Relevant Sessions

- 2026-04-09 — first confirmed as active target in durable memory.
- 2026-04-30 — interrogated after capture and reframed as a manipulated disposable worker rather than a prime mover.

## Open Questions

- Who is Jet Set Morgan really working for?
- How much autonomy does he actually have under the root-kitted control architecture wrapped around him?
- Could he become a useful witness/contact against the broader network, or is he too compromised?
