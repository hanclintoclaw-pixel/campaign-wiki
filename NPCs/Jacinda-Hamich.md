# Jacinda Hamich

## Overview

Record executive directly tied to the Darla Ledue fallout and one of the first non-runner civilians the crew briefed after the 612 Wharf / Site 7 operation started collapsing.

## Known Facts

- Jacinda is a record executive connected to the **SC Music** side of the Darla job.
- By **2026-05-28**, the crew met or contacted her directly to explain that Darla's masters had been stolen and folded into a much larger manipulation scandal.
- She was warned that the fallout would likely hit both **Darla Ledue** and **SC Music** hard once the operation began unraveling.
- She reacted with gratitude, but the runners came away thinking she did not fully grasp how ugly or wide the conspiracy really was.
- During the immediate aftermath, she briefly sheltered **Darla** and then prepared to go to ground with her once public pressure started building.

## Relationships

- Linked to **[Darla Ledue](Darla-Ledue.md)** as executive / handler / protector figure in the current arc.
- Linked to **[SC Music](../Factions/SC-Music.md)** through the stolen-masters job and its fallout.
- Linked to the crew as an informed civilian-side aftermath contact rather than a formal runner contact.

## Relevant Sessions

- 2026-05-28 — brought into the aftermath briefing; warned about the conspiracy and pushed toward concealment/flight with Darla.

## Open Questions

- How much did Jacinda know before the crew briefed her?
- Is she merely a worried executive, or was she closer to the compromised business layer than she admits?
- Where do she and Darla go now that the scandal is breaking?
