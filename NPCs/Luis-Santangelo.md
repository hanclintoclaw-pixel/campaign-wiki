# Luis Santangelo

## Overview

A local criminal power with community backing, brutal reputation, and an open-secret identity as a were-bear.

## Known Facts

- Luis Santangelo is described as a **were-bear**.
- He is presented as an **aspiring crime lord**.
- Historical source text describes him as a 7'3" half-Dominican / half-Ukrainian giant.
- He is known locally for a community focus paired with a terrifying reputation.
- It is an open secret that he eats his enemies.
- The local Hispanic community reveres him as a kind of bloodthirsty, terrifying Robin Hood.

## Relationships

- Linked to the local criminal ecology of Nashville.
- Linked to community-facing power structures as well as fear-based reputation.
