# Nashville

Nashville is a **city in the Confederated American States (CAS)**.

## SR3 canon status

At present, **direct Nashville-specific Shadowrun 3rd Edition canon appears sparse** in the local SR3 corpus. This page currently captures only what has been explicitly verified from SR3-era material outside the campaign.

## Verified SR3-era references

- In *Target: Smuggler Havens*, Nashville appears in a North American location list/map-style reference alongside other major cities. The extracted text places **Nashville under the UCAS listing in that source excerpt**, which may reflect OCR/layout ambiguity rather than settled setting geography, so that specific placement should be treated cautiously until checked against the original page image.
- In *State of the Art: 2064*, Nashville is mentioned in passing in a shadowtalk anecdote involving **BTLs being smuggled from Nashville** via a **CAS diplomatic pouch** into Boston street distribution.

## What can be said safely from those references

- Nashville exists as a recognized urban point of reference in SR3-era material.
- It is associated, at minimum indirectly, with illicit trade activity in one SR3-era reference.
- The current local corpus does **not yet provide a dense official city writeup** comparable to major Shadowrun setting hubs.

This page is intentionally conservative for now. As more verified SR3-era citations are found, they can be added here before or alongside campaign-specific Nashville material.
