# Atlas's

## Overview

Bar and private club just outside Nashville city limits that functions as a neutral gathering place and hiring center for the local middle-tier criminal and shadowrunner scene.

## Known Facts

- Atlas's is a freestanding two-story structure just outside the city limits of Nashville.
- It provides the middle tier of the local criminal community a neutral place to gather.
- The lower floor includes a small bar area with seating for about 30, a kitchen, and a private dining room with seating for 8.
- The upper floor includes three private rooms, each with seating for 8, and a small room used by **Ivan Toklas**.
- The local Shadowrunner community uses Atlas's as a hiring center.
- Historical sessions show the crew returning there as a staging and regroup point.

## Relationships

- Linked to **Ivan Toklas**.
- Linked to **Friendly Phyllis**.
- Linked to **Hardwire Henry**.
- Embedded in the broader Nashville shadowrunner and criminal ecology.
