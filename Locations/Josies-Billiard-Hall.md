# Josie's Billiard Hall

## Overview

Smoky Nashville dive bar / billiards hall used as **Righteous Haze's** regular hangout during his decline from retired fixer into decadent local predator.

## Known Facts

- Haze spent long nights at Josie's drinking, gambling, loan-sharking patrons, and holding court with hangers-on.
- The bar served low-rent drinks to most patrons, while Haze favored imported **Pyramid / Pharaoh beer** as a status flex and sealed-bottle safety preference.
- Kurgan and Curtis surveilled Haze there before manipulating his pickup routine.
- Curtis bought drinks and entertained Haze with submarine stories to build enough trust for the extraction.
- After Haze was killed, Kurgan nailed Haze's distinctive cyberhand to the door as a warning that management had changed.

## Relationships

- Regular hangout of **[Righteous Haze](../NPCs/Righteous-Haze.md)**.
- Connected to **[Kurgan](../PCs/Kurgan.md)** and **[Curtis](../PCs/Curtis.md)** through the 2066-05-09 interlude.
