# Huntsville

## Overview

**Huntsville** is a city in the **[Confederated American States](Confederated-American-States.md)** and the headquarters city of **[The Princeps Group](../Factions/The-Princeps-Group.md)**.

## Campaign Role

- Huntsville anchors Princeps as a CAS domestic military-technology actor rather than a Nashville-local corporation.
- Its defense-research identity makes it a natural home for a protected miltech company with strong CAS military ties.
- The city's importance in the current campaign record is primarily through Princeps, Claude August, and the legal/political stakes around domestic control of strategic military research.

## Key Organizations

- [The Princeps Group](../Factions/The-Princeps-Group.md) - independent single-A miltech research corporation.

## Key People

- [Claude August](../NPCs/Claude-August.md) - CEO and controlling owner of Princeps.
