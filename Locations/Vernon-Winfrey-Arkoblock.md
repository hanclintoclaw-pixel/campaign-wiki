# Vernon Winfrey Arkoblock

## Overview

The **Vernon Winfrey arkoblock** is the large residential tower adjacent to [Happy Cat Konpeki and Collectibles](Happy-Cat-Konpeki-and-Collectibles.md). During the Pixel Sticks investigation, it became the likely physical side of the gang's Matrix infrastructure after Curtis spotted a laser modem link from Happy Cat's roof into a blacked-out upper-floor window.

## Description

- Large, dense, run-down East Nashville residential tower looming over the smaller Happy Cat storefront.
- The lower atrium contains shops, building-management areas, a miserable water feature, crowds, and ordinary arkoblock foot traffic.
- Several floors appear to be socially or criminally claimed rather than cleanly controlled by building management.
- Public elevators to some upper floors are blocked or watched by youths and gang-adjacent personnel.

## Known Details

- A laser modem receiver/transmitter pair links Happy Cat's roof to a blacked-out window in the tower.
- Looking through that receiving window, the crew saw electronics consistent with server racks or Matrix infrastructure.
- Pixel Sticks iconography appears around the **20th, 21st, and 22nd floors**.
- At least one unknown non-decker gang or criminal group also has tags or personnel in the same stack of floors.
- The stairwells contain improvised filters and barricades, including a light barricade around the 10th floor and a more robust sealed barricade just below the 20th floor.
- Past the 20th-floor barricade, the stairwell looked dusty and little-used; Kurgan estimated no one had passed through that route for weeks.
- Four strange silver discs were visible up the stairs beyond the 20th-floor approach.

## Security / Access

- Public elevator access to the suspected Pixel Sticks floors is not free-flowing.
- Stair access is possible but physically tiring, narrow, and partially blocked by improvised barricades.
- Several floor doors have DIY electronic security systems, likely installed by criminal groups, self-defense cliques, or local occupants.
- The crew bypassed the stronger barricade by having Curtis kick a weak low corner open and sending Mr. Clean through to unlatch it from the inside.
