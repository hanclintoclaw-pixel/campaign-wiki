# Hidden Enforcers Oath Floor

## Overview

**The Oath Floor** is the Hidden Enforcers' secret initiatory headquarters beneath [Seven Directions Dojo](Seven-Directions-Dojo.md). Publicly, the lower level is a condemned basement and storm-shelter remnant. In practice, it is a warded meeting hall, oath chamber, training floor, and records vault used for private Hidden Enforcers business.

## Stub details

- Hidden below the public dojo through a locked stairwell concealed behind mat storage.
- Built into older reinforced basement infrastructure, likely predating the current business.
- Used for initiatory meetings, oath instruction, controlled sparring, and confidential civic-enforcement briefings.
- Maintained as a deliberately plain space: concrete, old brass fixtures, shielded lockers, and a central mat marked with a seven-point compass motif.
- The site is meant to feel less like a temple and more like a precinct basement that learned how to keep magical secrets.

## Connected people and groups

- Secret headquarters site for the **[Hidden Enforcers](../Factions/Hidden-Enforcers.md)**.
- Physically concealed beneath **[Seven Directions Dojo](Seven-Directions-Dojo.md)**.
- Potential route for **[Valgaut](../PCs/Valgaut.md)** if his Hidden Enforcers contact develops into formal initiation.

## Security / access

- Access requires sponsorship or explicit invitation from a trusted Hidden Enforcers member.
- Mundane concealment is layered with social screening through the dojo cover business.
- Magical and Matrix security are intentionally unspecified until the site appears in play.
