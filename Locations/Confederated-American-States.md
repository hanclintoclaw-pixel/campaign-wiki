# Confederated American States

The **Confederated American States (CAS)** is the national frame for several campaign locations and institutions, including Nashville and Huntsville. In the Princeps material, the CAS government protects a domestic miltech corporation as a counterweight to international megacorporate influence.

## Cities

- [Huntsville](Huntsville.md) - headquarters city of [The Princeps Group](../Factions/The-Princeps-Group.md).
- [Nashville](Nashville.md)

## Relevant Organizations

- [The Princeps Group](../Factions/The-Princeps-Group.md) - independent Huntsville-based miltech corporation with strong CAS military ties.

## Relevant People

- [Claude August](../NPCs/Claude-August.md) - CEO and controlling owner of Princeps; his secret dragon identity may imperil his legal standing under CAS citizenship and ownership law.
