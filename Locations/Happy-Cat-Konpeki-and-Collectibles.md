# Happy Cat Konpeki and Collectibles

## Overview

Independent convenience store and collectibles shop on Vernon Winfrey Avenue in Nashville. Happy Cat Konpeki and Collectibles has become a neighborhood hangout for local teens: part snack stop, part trading-card counter, part place to loiter where the clerks know when to pretend not to notice.

The store is small, bright, and stubbornly local, nestled between the huge arkoblocks that rise around it. Against all that concrete mass, Happy Cat reads like a pocket of neon clutter and teenage noise that the surrounding towers never quite managed to swallow.
