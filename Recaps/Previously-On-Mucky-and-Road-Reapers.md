# Previously On: Mucky and the Road Reapers

## Short version

- By the later archive, **Cindy Lou Prime** was hidden in Taco's basement while the crew also got pulled into Road Reaper politics.
- A large bounty on **Mucky** made it clear he mattered to the Road Reapers for reasons beyond simple money.
- The Road Reaper world revolved around a roadhouse culture with hard internal rules, recognizable colors, and escalating power pressure.
- After **Jerry-Can Jerry** was killed, the internal succession picture sharpened, with figures like **Alanzo Sparks** emerging as serious contenders.
- The crew's outside-town work increasingly overlapped with Road Reaper politics, Mucky's maneuvering, and adjacent chop-shop / criminal infrastructure.
