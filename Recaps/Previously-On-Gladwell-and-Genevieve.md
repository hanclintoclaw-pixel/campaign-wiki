# Previously On: Gladwell and Genevieve

## Short version

- The crew first got tangled in a case involving embarrassing leaks tied to the **Gladwell Institute**, a rehab facility serving high-profile clients.
- Suspicion focused on internal security and medical figures, especially **Eugene Booker** and **Dr. Martin Waters**.
- Later, the archive returned to similar exploitation themes through **Genevieve Watson**, a missing employee whose cyberware had apparently been rigged for covert recording without informed consent.
- The through-line is ugly and very consistent: vulnerable people being used as technology platforms while other actors try to recover the data, hardware, or leverage hidden inside them.
