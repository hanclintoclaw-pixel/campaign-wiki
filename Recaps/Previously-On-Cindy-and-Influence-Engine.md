# Previously On: Cindy and the Influence-Engine Arc

## Short version

- The crew got pulled into a job around **Cindy**, a knowbot performer tied to Swiftwing Records and later to broader manipulation/influence systems.
- Hardware trails, fenced matrix infrastructure, and shady intermediaries like **Blue Rayven** and **6 Steel Strings** suggested Cindy was never just a missing singer.
- Over time, the Cindy thread blurred into questions of copied identity, behavioral manipulation, audience engineering, and hidden control systems.
- By the later archive, **Cindy Lou Prime** was being actively contained and protected in Taco's basement while the crew tried to keep her functional but limited.
- The modern 2026 arc continues to echo this material through **Darla**, media influence, and surveillance-heavy music-industry actors.
