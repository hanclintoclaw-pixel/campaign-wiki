# Previously On: Mall Conflict

## Short version

- The crew took a job around a supposedly abandoned pre-collapse mall that outside interests wanted cleared, verified, and secured.
- Almost immediately it became obvious the site was not empty: people were living there, infrastructure was active, and multiple factions had a stake in the place.
- The **Steel Backs** emerged as the main factional concern, while **Vox Populi** raised the stakes by implying the site mattered as a hacker/infrastructure nexus, not just real estate.
- The result is less a simple eviction/security job and more a contested urban-territory problem involving redevelopment pressure, squatters, gang actors, and strategic physical infrastructure.
