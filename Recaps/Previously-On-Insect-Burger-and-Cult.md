# Previously On: Insect Burger and Cult Arc

## Short version

- The crew first took what looked like a counterfeit-food / fake-Stuffer-Shack job.
- Very quickly, the site started to look wrong in a much deeper way: surveillance-heavy, data-hungry, cultish, and tied to strange magical ingredients.
- The raw materials turned out to be magically active even when the finished product looked mundane.
- The horror underneath the scam was that insect-spirit material was being processed into intoxicants and food products.
- By the hunting-lodge stage, captives were being used to summon insect spirits, which were then harvested and processed into burger meat.
