# Previously On: Dragon Egg and Ghostwalker

## Short version

- What began as a job to recover dangerous toxic magical biomaterial turned into something much stranger.
- The crew followed eggshell evidence, odd surveillance, and the testimony of a disturbed old man who had found a giant egg in the woods.
- Outside pressure mounted, including evidence that the crew's egg-related materials were being tracked.
- Eventually the arc escalated into an overt dragon-egg and hybrid-creature problem.
- By the time the pieces were coming together, the situation seemed linked not just to weird local magic but to **Ghostwalker-level** draconic stakes.
