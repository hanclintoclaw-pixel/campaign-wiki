# Uruk Hai

## Overview

An orcish / trollish go-gang operating around the **Pie-town Happening** arc. The recovered archive presents them as the protective muscle around a magically-charged public event linked to skooma and hidden infrastructure.

## What the crew knows

- They were protecting the Pie-town Happening.
- Their presence that close to the city center was notable and risky for them.
- The event they were tied to had both magical significance and criminal-commercial layers.
