# Road Reapers

## Overview

A gang / road-house-centered power structure operating on the outside of town. In the recovered archive, they sit at the center of the **Mucky** pressure arc and the post-**Jerry-Can Jerry** succession scramble.

## What the crew knows

- They maintain a rules-heavy roadhouse environment with clear chain-of-command expectations.
- They were willing to post or tolerate a substantial bounty on **Mucky**.
- Internal leadership pressure increased after Jerry-Can Jerry was killed.
- **Razor Jake** and **Alanzo Sparks** emerged as notable figures in the power landscape.
