# Ace Freelits

## Overview

A low-rent motorcycle-gang / disposable-muscle faction found working the warehouse layer of the 612 Wharf counterfeit-goods operation.

## Known Facts

- Ace Freelits personnel were seen doing manual labor at **612 Wharf Avenue**.
- They were boxing and shipping counterfeit **Meatlets** and **Bebop Cola** and preparing poster-swap work for the upcoming **Darla-Doo** release push.
- Session evidence frames them as **low-level contract labor / gangers**, not the real strategic brains behind the operation.
- By the warehouse breach, surviving rank-and-file workers largely fled rather than standing as a disciplined core faction.

## Relationships

- Linked to **[612 Wharf Avenue and Site 7](../Locations/612-Wharf-and-Site-7.md)** as the visible warehouse-labor layer.
- Linked indirectly to **[Mother Yahweh](../NPCs/Mother-Yahweh.md)** and the counterfeit-food pipeline.
- Adjacent to the broader Darla / counterfeit-promo / influence-network arc.

## Relevant Sessions

- 2026-05-07 — first clearly observed at the warehouse doing labor and packaging work.
- 2026-05-14 — still understood as the visible labor layer while the deeper site was uncovered.
- 2026-05-21 — remaining warehouse labor broke and ran during the breach aftermath.
