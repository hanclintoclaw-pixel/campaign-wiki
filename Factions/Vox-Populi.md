# Vox Populi

## Overview

A hacker group associated with data trafficking and intrusion. In the mall arc, their presence suggests the site matters not just as real estate but as a nexus of useful infrastructure.

## What the crew knows

- Some people at the mall are associated with Vox Populi.
- The group is tied to hacking, intrusion, and data-oriented activity.
- The mall was selected in part because of its physical infrastructure value.
