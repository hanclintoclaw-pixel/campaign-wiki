# Draco Foundation

## Overview

Major magical-research / intervention institution with a strong reputation for addressing dangerous magical problems.

## Known Facts

- The Draco Foundation is treated like a major NGO/research institution dealing with magical trouble.
- It has a generally positive reputation, though some people suspect hidden agendas.
- It does not have formal remit to operate openly in the CAS.
- Kari Wilkerson represented Foundation interests in Nashville as a private citizen.

## Relationships

- Linked to **Kari Wilkerson**.
- Linked to the toxic magical biomaterial / egg-fragment investigation.
- Compared and contrasted with the **Hidden Enforcers** and federal authorities.
