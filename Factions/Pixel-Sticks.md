# Pixel Sticks

## Overview

The **Pixel Sticks** are a Matrix youth gang or tag associated with recent vandalism and disruption across Nashville's telecom grid. The crew has not yet confirmed their membership or leadership, but Mevin's host run at Happy Cat found a hidden Pixel Sticks tag yard, and the physical trail now points into the adjacent Vernon Winfrey arkoblock.

## Known Facts

- The Pixel Sticks are associated with petty but widespread Matrix vandalism, including disruption of user logs, delivery systems, and local telecom services.
- Their work carries bright/neon iconography and a recognizable tag.
- They appear to be using extremely advanced utilities that should be beyond ordinary youth-gang capability.
- The utilities may be burning out quickly because the group is spamming them across real targets.
- A pattern in the vandalism wave points toward **[Happy Cat Konpeki and Collectibles](../Locations/Happy-Cat-Konpeki-and-Collectibles.md)** because the store was conspicuously not hit.
- The Happy Cat host hides a Pixel Sticks tag yard with utility crates, dead drops, route maps, burner personas, reputation tags, and job sorting.
- A laser modem connects Happy Cat's roof to a blacked-out window in the **[Vernon Winfrey Arkoblock](../Locations/Vernon-Winfrey-Arkoblock.md)**.
- Pixel Sticks tags appear around the 20th, 21st, and 22nd floors of the arkoblock, though another unknown gang or criminal group also appears to share the same floor stack.
- The crew has not yet proven whether the Pixel Sticks are the true holders of the utilities, useful idiots, protected relatives, patsies, or a front for another actor.

## Current Crew Read

Mevin suspects the client or source is hiding something because the dossier has edited monitoring gaps and because the recovery bounty is much lower than the theoretical value of pristine high-end utilities. Kurgan reads the Johnson as a very serious corporate cutout, meaning the sponsor has resources and wants distance. After the Happy Cat host run, the crew also knows the opposition noticed a server shutdown but did not trace Mevin.
