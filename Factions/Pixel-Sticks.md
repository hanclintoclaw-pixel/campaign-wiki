# Pixel Sticks

## Overview

The **Pixel Sticks** are a Matrix youth gang or tag associated with recent vandalism and disruption across Nashville's telecom grid. The crew has not yet confirmed their membership, leadership, or exact hangout, but Mevin's review of the Johnson's dossier tied the name to the current rash of neon-styled Matrix vandalism.

## Known Facts

- The Pixel Sticks are associated with petty but widespread Matrix vandalism, including disruption of user logs, delivery systems, and local telecom services.
- Their work carries bright/neon iconography and a recognizable tag.
- They appear to be using extremely advanced utilities that should be beyond ordinary youth-gang capability.
- The utilities may be burning out quickly because the group is spamming them across real targets.
- A pattern in the vandalism wave points toward **[Happy Cat Konpeki and Collectibles](../Locations/Happy-Cat-Konpeki-and-Collectibles.md)** because the store was conspicuously not hit.
- The crew has not yet proven whether the Pixel Sticks are the true holders of the utilities, useful idiots, protected relatives, patsies, or a front for another actor.

## Current Crew Read

Mevin suspects the client or source is hiding something because the dossier has edited monitoring gaps and because the recovery bounty is much lower than the theoretical value of pristine high-end utilities. Kurgan reads the Johnson as a very serious corporate cutout, meaning the sponsor has resources and wants distance.

## Open Questions

- Who leads the Pixel Sticks?
- Who supplied or lost the advanced utilities?
- Are the Pixel Sticks acting independently, being sponsored, or being set up?
- What is their relationship to Happy Cat?
- Are they physically present among the teen/decker crowds near Happy Cat, or only using that area as a Matrix landmark?
