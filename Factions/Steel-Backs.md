# Steel Backs

## Overview

A major faction tied to the contested mall arc. The recovered archive presents them as the primary heavy factional concern at the mall, more significant than the Mall Rats and entangled with the site's infrastructure value.

## What the crew knows

- Their name surfaced in connection with the mall security/redevelopment job.
- They appear to be one of the main forces the crew is expected to deal with at the site.
- Their interests overlap with a location valued for its physical infrastructure and strategic utility.

## Alias note

The archive alternates between **Steelback** and **Steel Backs**. This page normalizes the public-facing name as **Steel Backs** while preserving **Steelback** as an alias.
