# The Princeps Group

## Overview

**The Princeps Group** is an independent **single-A corporation** headquartered in **[Huntsville](../Locations/Huntsville.md)** in the **[Confederated American States](../Locations/Confederated-American-States.md)**. Princeps is a military-technology research corporation with unusually strong ties to the CAS military, protected by the CAS government as a domestic counterweight to larger international megacorporate influence.

## What the Crew Knows

- Princeps is a Huntsville-based miltech research corporation rather than a megacorporate subsidiary.
- The corporation has strong military, defense-research, and procurement ties inside the CAS.
- CAS protection gives Princeps room to remain independent despite pressure from more powerful international corporate actors.
- The current owner and CEO is **[Claude August](../NPCs/Claude-August.md)**.
- Claude came to power after the prior leadership became embroiled in a financial scandal.
- The takeover was a government-backed stock acquisition, suggesting CAS authorities preferred Claude's control to either the old leadership or outside acquisition.
- Players know Claude is secretly a juvenile western dragon; Princeps's public/legal position depends on that truth staying contained.

## Public Face

Princeps presents itself as a patriotic CAS defense innovator: technically ambitious, nationally useful, and safer in domestic hands than under Ares, Saeder-Krupp, Wuxing, or other extranational corporate pressure.

## Actual Function

Princeps gives the CAS a protected research and procurement channel in a sector where foreign megacorps would otherwise dominate. That makes it both valuable and politically sensitive: the company's independence is not merely corporate branding, but part of a national-security posture.

## Assets / Reach

- Huntsville headquarters and research base.
- Miltech research programs with defense applications.
- Access to CAS military patronage, procurement influence, and political cover.
- A corporate ownership structure recently reshaped by scandal and a state-backed takeover.

## Key People

- **[Claude August](../NPCs/Claude-August.md)** - young CEO and controlling owner; secretly a juvenile western dragon.

## Relationships

- **[Confederated American States](../Locations/Confederated-American-States.md):** Patron, protector, and likely major customer.
- **CAS military:** Close research and procurement relationship.
- **International megacorps:** Strategic rivals and acquisition threats, especially in miltech.
- **Former Princeps leadership:** Displaced after a financial scandal created the opening for Claude's takeover.
