# Heavenly Host

## Overview

An apparently old, mostly extinct Matrix-oriented youth gang/faction whose iconography unexpectedly resurfaced during the **Alvin Flang** surveillance job.

## What the crew knows

- **Kurgan** recognized the insignia on **[Diego](../NPCs/Diego.md)** as belonging to **Heavenly Host**.
- The faction was remembered as a mostly **Matrix-oriented youth gang** that had been effectively gone for years.
- Their historical profile seemed closer to Matrix harassment, petty digital trouble, graffiti, and nuisance activity than to polished modern corporate espionage.
- That makes Diego's presence especially strange: he looked more like active street muscle/cybersam protection than a washed-up vandal wearing retro colors for fun.
